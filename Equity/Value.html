Features of a Value Mutual Fund

  • Long-Term Investment: Value funds are suitable for investors with a long-term investment horizon.

How Does a Value Mutual Fund Work?

Value fund managers look for stocks that are inexpensive in terms of earnings, including recent self-corrections. These funds may pick stocks that are out of favour, but have strong fundamentals. The managers identify potentially undervalued stocks with intrinsic value and invest in them with the expectation that their value will increase in the future.
