Features and Benefits of YES Bank FD

  • The tenure ranges from 7 days to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit amount is ₹10,000. No maximum limit
  • Interest is compounded quarterly and paid as per the chosen frequency
  • Senior citizens receive an additional 0.50% interest rate
  • Nomination facility is available for all FDs
  • Loan/overdraft facility is available up to 90% of the deposit amount
  • Auto-renewal facility is available for all FDs
  • Tax-saving FDs are available for 5-year tenures, with tax deduction under Section 80C
  • Latest YES Bank FD interest rates can be accessed from YES Bank's official website

Types of YES Bank FD Schemes

Resident Fixed Deposit

  • Standard deposit scheme for all customers, tenure ranges from 7 days to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit: ₹10,000
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)
  • Special rates for senior citizens

Yes Bank Tax Saver FD

  • 5-year lock-in period, eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961
  • Maximum investment: ₹1.5 lakh per annum
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest paid quarterly or on maturity

Yes Bank Senior Citizens FD Scheme

  • Special deposit scheme for senior citizens, offers extra interest rates and benefits
  • Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)

Term-wise YES Bank Fixed Deposit Rates

Short-term Deposits

YES Bank FDs have tenures ranging from 7 days to 12 months, the interest rate ranges from 3.25% to 6.10% p.a. on deposits. These deposits are best for investors looking for lower tenure FDs and liquidity.

Medium-term Deposits

Medium-term deposits have tenures ranging from 1 year to less than 5 years. These FDs offer higher rates. 1 year YES Bank FD rate is 7.25%.

Long-term Deposits

These deposits have tenures ranging from 5 years to 10 years and offer a rate of 7.25%.

How to Invest in YES Bank FD?

Here are the ways through which one can open a Yes Bank FD Account:

1) NetBanking

  • Visit Yes Bank's website and sign in to retail net banking using your login and password. After signing in, head towards deposits and select your account. Open Yes Bank Deposit section.
  • Next, select tenure, deposit amount, deposit area, maturity, Nominee is Required, Sweep in Required and submit.
  • After completing, you will receive a confirmation number and your FD will get booked.

2) Mobile App – Yes Mobile

  • Login to Yes Mobile App
  • Choose Deposit Section
  • Choose Fixed Deposit option
  • Choose Tenure, Deposit Amount, Deposit Area, Tenure, Maturity instructions, Nomination Required, Sweep in Required and submit.
  • After completing, you will receive a confirmation message.

3) Desktop (Web Mode)

  • Go to Yes Bank NetBanking or Yes Bank website.
  • Search for Yes Bank on Facebook Messenger or click the "Yes Robot" icon present at the bottom right of the website.
  • Type "Open Fixed Deposit" and follow the steps as required.
  • After completing, you will receive a confirmation message online.

4) Offline Mode

  • Call 1800 1200
  • Choose "Savings Account related query" and talk to FD Help.
  • Choose an option to visit the nearby branch or create and book a fixed deposit.

5) Booking Through WhatsApp

  • Send "Hi" to 8291201200 through a mobile number registered with the Bank.
  • Choose Yes Bank Fixed Deposit, then share all fixed FD booking queries.
  • Enter tenure, maturity, and other details.
  • Receive confirmation by chat.
  • Submit Nominee to book your FD.

Eligibility to Open a YES Bank FD Account

All individuals belonging to any of the following categories can open a Yes Bank FD online:

  • Resident individuals, including those (legal guardians and as co-applicant)
  • Hindu undivided families
  • Senior citizens
  • Minors under resident individuals
  • Public and private limited companies
  • Co-operative societies, clubs and associations

Documents Required to Open YES Bank FD

The following documents have to be submitted by applicants for account/activation of Yes Bank FD -

  • Recent passport-size photograph and identity proof, as per the list of documents below.
  • Valid ID document such as Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or driving license, as the proof of identification.
  • Address proof, such as bank statement or utility bill, as the proof of address.
  • PAN card, as per the bank's requirements.
  • Other documents, such as certificate of business/trust to be submitted accordingly.
  • The authorized signatories, certificate of authorization must be submitted accordingly.
  • Co-operative societies, clubs, and associations must submit a copy of their by-laws.

Tax on Yes Bank Fixed Deposit

An investment in the Yes Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. on the other Yes Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the Yes Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from Yes Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a. For senior citizens, the limit is Rs. 50,000 p.a. Also, if the investor fails to provide PAN Card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against Yes Bank FD

Yes Bank provides loans against their Fixed Deposits. Investors can get up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan against the fixed deposit. YES Bank allows loans at the rate between 4.5% - 6.5% above the FD rate, as per the bank's rules.

Premature Closure of Yes Bank Fixed Deposit

YES Bank offers a premature withdrawal facility to its investors. The penalty is levied as per Yes Bank's terms and conditions. For FDs above Rs. 5 lakhs, the premature withdrawal penalty is 0.50% for senior citizens and 1.00% for fixed deposits above Rs. 5 lakh, as per the prevailing rules. Withdrawals made after 1 year will be calculated based on the deposit repayment amount and the interest rate for the relevant period, as per the bank's rules.
