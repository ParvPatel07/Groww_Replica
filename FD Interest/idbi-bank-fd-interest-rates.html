Features and Benefits of IDBI Fixed Deposit

  • Senior citizens get an additional interest rate of 0.50% p.a. over regular rates.
  • The minimum deposit required to open an FD with IDBI Bank is Rs. 10,000.
  • Nomination facility is provided by the bank.
  • Loan up to 90% of the deposited amount is available.
  • Interest is paid quarterly or monthly as per customer choice.
  • IDBI Bank provides multiple deposit schemes to its customers, including Suvidha Tax Saving FD, IDBI Bank Freedom Deposit, and more.

Types of IDBI FD Schemes

IDBI Bank Freedom Deposit

  • This scheme allows customers to invest in a range of higher interest rates, as per the deposit.
  • Minimum deposit: Rs. 10,000
  • Tenure: 7 days to 20 years
  • Quarterly or monthly interest payout option
  • Loan/overdraft facility up to 90% of the deposit
  • Nomination facility available
  • Senior citizens get additional interest

Suvidha Tax Saving Fixed Deposit

  • This is a tax-saving deposit scheme, designed for the resident individuals and HUFs who want to save and get tax-efficient returns.
  • Lock-in period of 5 years
  • Eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C
  • Maximum investment limit of â‚¹1.5 lakhs per year
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest rates as per regular FD
  • Quarterly compounding of interest
  • Nomination facility available
  • Senior citizens get additional interest

Term wise IDBI Fixed Deposit Rates

Short-term Deposits

  • Tenure ranging from 7 days to 1 year. No lock-in period, interest offered at 3.00% to 5.75% p.a. for both regular and senior citizens.

Medium-term Deposits

  • Tenure ranging from above 1 year to below 5 years, and offered at 6.80% to 7.25%.

Long-term Deposits

  • These deposits have deposit tenure ranging from 5 years to 20 years and offer a rate of 6.00% - 6.50%.

How to invest in IDBI FD?

An IDBI Bank FD account can be opened through different modes like Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Internet Banking

  • Sign in to IDBI Bank NetBanking account.
  • Navigate to the 'Fixed Deposit' option.
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information, and furnish maturity instructions.
  • After successful booking, the FD account will get booked and confirmation will be displayed.

2) Mobile App - IDBI Bank App

  • Download the IDBI Bank App and sign in.
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option.
  • Fill out all the required details, nominee information, and maturity booking as required.
  • After successful booking, the FD account will get booked and confirmation will be displayed.

3) Opening IDBI FD via Offline

For opening an IDBI Bank FD account via offline mode, visit the nearest IDBI Bank branch. Fill out the IDBI Bank FD application form and submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the IDBI Bank.

Eligibility to Open an IDBI FD Account

  • Individuals, joint and joint account holders
  • Minors (under guardianship)
  • Hindu Undivided Family (HUF) accounts
  • Resident Indians, NRI and NRO accounts
  • Minors (resident, NRI) with guardian
  • Senior citizens
  • Trusts, societies and partnerships
  • Companies, clubs and charitable trusts
  • Proprietorships and charitable trusts

Documents Required to Open IDBI FD

Furnishing proper identity and address proof is mandatory for applying for IDBI Bank FD. Minors and senior citizens also need to supply proof of age. List of documents necessary -

Proof of identity (any one)

  • PAN card
  • Voter ID card
  • Driving license
  • Passport
  • Senior citizen card (if applicable)

Proof of address (any one)

  • Electricity bill
  • Bank account statement with a cheque
  • Telephone bill
  • An ID card/certificate issued by Post Office

Applicants must provide originals, along with self-attested photocopies, when applying. Minors must also submit age proof, such as a birth certificate, passport, or school certificate, and senior citizens must provide age proof for availing senior citizen benefits.

Tax on IDBI Bank Fixed Deposit

An investment in IDBI Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other IDBI Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the IDBI Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from IDBI Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a. (Rs. 50,000 p.a. for senior citizens). If the investor fails to provide PAN Card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against IDBI Bank FD

IDBI Bank provides loans against their Fixed Deposits. Investors can get up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan against IDBI Bank Fixed Deposit. The rate of interest for the loan will be between 1-2% higher than the FD rate. However, for senior citizens, the differential rate is 1% above the FD rate. This loan is not available for tax-saving FDs.

Premature Closure of IDBI Bank Fixed Deposit

IDBI Bank Fixed Deposits can be withdrawn prematurely except for the tax-saving Fixed Deposit. However, if the bank charges a penalty of 1% on the applicable rate for such premature withdrawals. The penalty is applicable for all deposits above Rs. 5 lakh. For deposits below Rs. 5 lakh, no penalty is levied. Note that on tax-saving fixed deposits, premature withdrawal is not allowed.
