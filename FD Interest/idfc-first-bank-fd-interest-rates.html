Features and Benefits of IDFC First Bank FD

  • The minimum amount required to book an IDFC First Bank FD is Rs. 10,000, up to no limit.
  • Tenure ranges from 7 days to 10 years.
  • IDFC First Bank FDs are available under the Deposit Insurance Scheme of RBI, which covers up to ₹ 5 lakh of total deposits per customer.
  • Senior citizens get an additional 0.50% interest rate over the regular rates.
  • Loan/overdraft facility is available.
  • Auto-renewal and nomination facility is available.
  • Monthly/quarterly interest payout is available.

Types of IDFC First Bank FD Schemes

Regular Fixed Deposits

  • Available for all customers, with standard quarterly compounding.
  • Minimum deposit: Rs. 10,000
  • Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
  • Quarterly or monthly interest payout option
  • Loan/overdraft facility up to 90% of the deposit
  • Nomination facility available
  • Senior citizens get additional interest

IDFC Bank Tax Saver Fixed Deposit Account

  • Lock-in period of 5 years
  • Eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C
  • Maximum investment limit of ₹1.5 lakhs per year
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest rates as per regular FD
  • Quarterly compounding of interest
  • Nomination facility available
  • Senior citizens get additional interest

Term wise IDFC First Bank FD Rates

Short-term Deposits

  • Tenure ranging from 7 days to 1 year. No lock-in period, interest offered at 3.00% to 5.75% p.a. for both regular and senior citizens.

Medium-term Deposits

  • Tenure ranging from above 1 year to below 5 years, and offered at 7.00% to 7.50%.

Long-term Deposits

  • These deposits have deposit tenure ranging from 5 years to 10 years and offer a rate of 7.00% - 7.50%.

How to invest in IDFC First Bank FD?

An IDFC First Bank FD account can be opened through different modes like Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Internet Banking

  • Sign in to the NetBanking account.
  • Navigate to the 'Fixed Deposit' option and select the type of deposit account.
  • Fill out all the necessary information, nominee details, and maturity instructions.
  • After successful booking, the FD account will get booked and confirmation will be displayed.

2) Mobile App - IDFC Bank App

  • Download the IDFC Bank App and sign in.
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option and select the type of deposit account.
  • Fill out all the required details, nominee information, and maturity booking as required.
  • After successful booking, the FD account will get booked and confirmation will be displayed.

3) Opening IDFC First Bank FD via Offline

For opening an IDFC First Bank FD account via offline mode, visit the nearest IDFC Bank branch. Fill out the IDFC Bank FD application form and submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the IDFC Bank.

Eligibility to Open an IDFC First FD Account

  • Individuals
  • Hindu Undivided Family (HUF)
  • Minors (under guardianship)
  • Resident individuals, including sole proprietorships, partnership firms, private and public companies
  • Trusts, societies, and associations
  • Clubs, associations, and charitable trusts
  • Government and semi-government institutions, educational institutions, and entities established under statute

Documents Required to Open IDFC First Bank FD

The following documents have to be furnished for opening an IDFC First Bank FD –

  • Proof of identity (PAN card, Voter ID, Driving license, Passport, Aadhar card)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement with cheque, telephone bill, Aadhar card)
  • Passport size photographs, age proof for minors and senior citizens, and other documents as required

Tax on IDFC First Bank Fixed Deposit

An investment in IDFC First Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other IDFC First Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the IDFC First Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from IDFC First Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a. (Rs. 50,000 p.a. for senior citizens). If the investor fails to provide PAN Card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against IDFC First Bank FD

IDFC First Bank provides loans against their Fixed Deposits. Investors can avail up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan against IDFC First Bank Fixed Deposit. The rate of interest for the loan will be between 1-2% higher than the FD rate. However, for senior citizens, the differential rate is 1% above the FD rate. This loan is not available for tax-saving FDs.

Premature Closure of IDFC First Bank Fixed Deposit

IDFC First Bank Fixed Deposits can be withdrawn prematurely, excluding the tax-saving fixed deposit. The bank charges a penalty of 1% on the applicable rate for such premature withdrawals. The penalty is applicable for all deposits above Rs. 5 lakh. For deposits below Rs. 5 lakh, no penalty is levied. Note that on tax-saving fixed deposits, premature withdrawal is not allowed.
