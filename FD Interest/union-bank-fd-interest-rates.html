How to Invest in Union Bank FD?

A Union Bank FD account can be opened through different modes like Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Internet Banking

  • Sign in to the Union Bank NetBanking account
  • Navigate to the Fixed Deposit section
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information, and maturity instructions
  • After completing all the details, process, click on Submit. On successful booking, a confirmation message will be generated.

2) Mobile App â€“ Union Bank of India App

  • Download the Union Bank app and sign in
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option and add all the relevant details, nominee information, and maturity instructions
  • On successful booking, a confirmation message will be generated.

3) Offline mode (Branch visit)

For opening a Union Bank of India FD account via offline mode, one has to visit the nearest Union Bank of India branch, get the application form, fill it out, and submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the Union Bank of India.

Eligibility to Open a Union Bank FD Account

Individuals who fulfill the following factors are eligible to open a fixed deposit account with Union Bank.

  • Resident individuals, holding an individual or joint account
  • Minors under guardianship of an HUF
  • Partnership firms
  • Public and Private Limited companies
  • Societies, clubs, and associations
  • Agencies, trusts and societies

However, keep in mind that the tax-saving FD schemes are only available to an individual or joint account holders. Agencies or companies cannot invest through those plans.

Tax on Union Bank FD

An investment in Union Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other Union Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the Union Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from Union Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a. For senior citizens, the limit is Rs. 50,000 p.a. Also, if the investor fails to provide PAN Card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against Union Bank FD

Union Bank provides loans against their Fixed Deposits. Investors can get up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan against Union Bank Fixed Deposit. The rate of interest will be between 1.5% - 2.0% above the FD rate.

Premature Closure of Union Bank Fixed Deposit

Union Bank Fixed Deposits can be withdrawn prematurely (except for the tax-saving Fixed Deposit). The Union Bank charges a penalty of 1% for premature withdrawal, and the premature withdrawal penalty for FDs above Rs. 5 lakh, or without that period, is subject to modification as per the prevailing rules.
