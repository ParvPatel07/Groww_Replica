About Axis Bank

Axis Bank Ltd. is a prominent Indian Banking and financial services company. Headquartered in Mumbai, India, it is registered as the third largest private sector bank in India. It offers a wide range of financial services under its retail banking services such as deposits, loans, and corporate banking services like Demat account equity capital markets and mutual fund investments, and international banking services.

Axis Bank provides multiple fixed deposit options to its customers to secure a certain part of their savings for a fixed tenure. The competitive Axis Bank Fixed Deposit interest rates help the depositors to earn more returns by safeguarding their money and to use them judiciously in the future. Customers have to deposit the minimum deposit amount at the time of opening the account. Furthermore, withdrawal of the FD amount is not allowed until the maturity period.

How to Invest in Axis Bank FD?

An Axis Bank FD account can be opened via different modes like Internet Banking and offline.

1) Open FD Account Through Axis Bank Internet Banking

Follow the listed down steps for opening an Axis Bank FD account via internet banking:

  • Log in to the Internet Banking account and select the 'Deposit' option.
  • Select 'Fixed Deposit' from the available options.
  • Furnish all the required information and examine details.
  • After confirming, fund requirements will be debited from the Savings Account to the Fixed Deposit account. Deposit will receive a web receipt of the fixed deposit immediately.

On successful booking, the Fixed Deposit advice will be sent out or a request for same in physical will be sent to the communication address. Also, depositor can download the FD advice via internet banking after one working day of booking the fixed deposit.

2) Open an Axis Bank FD Offline

To open an Axis Bank FD account offline, one needs to visit the nearest Axis Bank branch. Fill out the Axis Bank FD application form and submit all the required documents. Next, deposit the required funds to open the fixed deposit. On successful processing of the application, the FD receipt will be provided by the Axis Bank.

Eligibility to Open an Axis Bank FD Account

Individuals and companies satisfying any of the following criteria can successfully open an Axis Bank fixed deposit account:

  1. Resident individuals including minors.
  2. Hindu Undivided Families (HUF)
  3. Partnership firms, Proprietorship firms and Joint stock companies.
  4. Trusts.

Documents Required to Open Axis Bank FD Account

  1. Identification proof through KYC documents such as Aadhaar card, Driver's License and Voter ID card.
  2. PAN card as per rules established by the Income Tax Department of India.
  3. Address proof through KYC documents, Passport, and utility bills such as electricity and water bill.

Tax on Axis Bank Fixed Deposit

Axis Bank offers the Tax Saving FD at an attractive interest rate and a tenure of 5 years. The minimum and maximum deposit under this scheme are Rs.100 and Rs.1.5 lakh, respectively. At present, Axis Bank offers the rate of interest at 5.75% p.a.

The bank even offers several tax saving deposits and FD options like FD Plus, Regular Deposit, Encash 24 Fixed Deposit, and other options. These are suitable if you want to invest for a short time or withdraw your funds early.

Under the fixed deposit tax deduction benefit option, you can save up to Rs. 1,50,000 annually according to Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

Loan Against Axis Bank FD

Axis Bank customers can also avail of a loan against Axis fixed deposit account. The account holder can get up to 90% of the value of the FD as a loan.

Premature Closure of Axis Bank Fixed Deposit

You can easily withdraw the money from FD before maturity, subject to a penalty of 1.0%. Although, if you wish to withdraw the money only partially, the bank will levy no penalty on the fixed portion withdrawn up to 25% of the principal amount.
