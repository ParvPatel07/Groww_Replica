How to Invest in UCO Bank FD?

For opening an FD account in UCO Bank, one needs to visit any nearest UCO Bank branch and request the application form for opening a fixed deposit. Fill out the application form, attach the required KYC documents, and submit the application along with the deposit amount. On successful processing, the UCO Bank FD account will be opened, and the FD receipt will be provided by the branch. UCO Bank FDs can also be opened online via internet banking, by logging in to the UCO Bank NetBanking portal, selecting the FD option, and following the process as per the instructions.

Eligibility to Open a UCO Bank FD Account

Individuals who fulfill the following factors are eligible to invest in UCO Bank FD schemes.

  • Indian citizen or NRI
  • Member of Hindu Undivided Family

Companies, trusts, and societies can also apply for a UCO Bank FD plan.

Documents Required to Open UCO Bank FD

Investors must furnish the following documents to enroll their UCO Bank investment.

  • KYC documents (as identity and address proof: Aadhaar card, PAN card, driving license, electricity bill)
  • Bank statement along with a cheque acts as the income proof
  • Passport-size photograph
  • UCO Bank fixed deposit application form

Tax on UCO Bank Fixed Deposit

Investment in UCO Bank Tax Saver Fixed Deposit is entitled to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Also, one can claim while filing income tax returns. Also, note that the rate of the UCO Bank Fixed Deposit, the interest income is subject to TDS of 10% if the income from UCO Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a. For senior citizens, the limit is Rs. 50,000 p.a. Also, if the investor fails to provide PAN Card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan against UCO Bank FD

UCO Bank allows customers to avail loan against their fixed deposits. However, this facility is available for the traditional fixed deposit schemes. For all other plans, the bank offers a loan amount up to 90% of the FD value. The interest rate on amount charged, if UCO Bank FD is availed, the interest rate on loans ranges from 1.5% to 2.5%.

Premature Closure of UCO Bank Fixed Deposit

UCO Bank permits premature withdrawal of most FDs. A penalty of 1% is levied below the contractual interest rate will be charged.
