An auto-renewal option is also provided, simplifying higher returns without the hassle of going to the branch.

Features and Benefits of IOB FD

  • The FD has a minimum deposit: 7 days to 10 years
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Quarterly compounding of interest
  • Premature withdrawal facility (with penalty)
  • Nomination facility available
  • Special rates for senior citizens

Types of IOB FD Schemes

Indian Overseas Regular Fixed Deposit Scheme

  • Offers standard FD rates for all customers. The deposit can be auto-renewed at the deposit holder's request, which offers flexibility and convenience.
  • Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit: ₹1,000
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)

Reinvestment Deposit

  • Interest is compounded quarterly and allowed to be reinvested and earned on total deposit.
  • Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit: ₹1,000
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)

IOB Equity Plus FD

  • Special deposit, typically alongside the super senior citizens of India (80+) and those looking to earn higher rates (0.5% p.a. extra)
  • Tenure: 1 year to 10 years
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)

Vardhan – Deposit of Senior Citizens

  • Additional interest for senior citizens, along with the super senior citizens (above 80 years) and those aged 60 years get 0.5% p.a. higher on all tenures.
  • Tenure: 7 days to 10 years
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)

Tax Saver Scheme

  • 5-year lock-in period, especially designated as tax-saving provision under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
  • Maximum investment: ₹1.5 lakh per annum
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest paid quarterly or on maturity

Term-wise Indian Overseas Bank FD Rates

Short-term Deposits

Short-term IOB FDs cover tenures ranging from 7 days to 12 months, best for investors who want a fixed return in 3 to 12 months. These deposits offer rates between 4.00% to 5.50%.

Medium-term Deposits

Medium-term IOB FDs have tenures from 1 year to less than 5 years. 1 year IOB FD rate is 6.00%.

Long-term Deposits

These deposits have tenures ranging from 5 to 10 years and offer a rate of around 5.75%.

How to Invest in Indian Overseas Bank FD?

You can invest in IOB FD through both online methods such as internet banking and mobile app, and offline modes as well.

1) Internet Banking

  • Sign in to IOB NetBanking account
  • Navigate to the Fixed Deposit section
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information, and maturity instructions
  • After completing all the details, click 'Open Deposit' and click Save to proceed, and fill all the necessary details along with savings information.
  • After saving, the account is created. Confirmation is made via SMS.

2) Mobile App – IOB Mobile App

  • Follow the below steps to open an FD account through IOB Mobile App:
  • Download the IOB Mobile App and sign in
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option, and select the 'Open Deposit' option to proceed.
  • Fill out all the required details, nominee, and maturity instructions to book a fixed deposit.
  • Lastly, verify all details and click on Confirm to book the fixed deposit.

3) Opening IOB FD Offline

For opening an IOB FD account offline, one needs to visit the nearest Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) branch, fill out a prescribed FD application form, and submit all necessary documents along with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the Indian Overseas Bank.

Eligibility to Open an IOB FD Account

Individuals or entities satisfying the following criteria can sign up for a fixed deposit in IOB bank -

  • Resident individuals, singly or jointly
  • Minors, if they have an applicant above the age of 18
  • HUFs, trusts, partnership firms, and private limited companies
  • Societies, associations, and public/private limited companies

Documents Required to Open Indian Overseas Bank FD

The following documents to have in hand for opening an IOB FD -

  • KYC documents for identification and address proof. You can submit any of the following documents for this:
    • PAN card
    • Aadhaar card
    • Passport
    • Voter ID
    • Driving License
  • Additionally, a cancelled cheque and signature must be furnished as address proof.

PAN card is for tax-related requirements.

Tax on Indian Overseas Bank Fixed Deposit

An investment in Indian Overseas Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other Indian Overseas Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the Indian Overseas Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from Indian Overseas Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a.

For senior citizens, the limit is Rs. 50,000 p.a. Also, if the investor fails to provide PAN card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against Indian Overseas Bank FD

Indian Overseas Bank offers loans against their FDs. Investors can get up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan against Indian Overseas Bank Fixed Deposit. The rate of interest will be between 1-2% higher than the FD rate. However, for senior citizens, the differential rate is 1% above the FD rate.

Premature Closure of Indian Overseas Bank Fixed Deposit

Indian Overseas Bank Fixed Deposits can be withdrawn prematurely, excluding the tax-saving Fixed Deposit. No penalty is levied for deposits below Rs. 5 lakhs if kept for 12 months. For fixed deposits greater than Rs. 5 lakhs or without that period, the penalty is levied as per bank rules.
