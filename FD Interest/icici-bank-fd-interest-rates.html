Features and Benefits of ICICI Fixed Deposit

  • Senior citizens get an additional interest rate of 0.50% p.a. over regular rates.
  • The minimum deposit required to open an FD with ICICI Bank is Rs. 10,000.
  • Nomination facility is provided by the bank.
  • Loan up to 90% of the deposited amount is available.
  • Interest is paid quarterly.
  • ICICI Bank provides multiple deposit schemes to its customers, including Regular FD, Tax Saving FD, Senior Citizen FD, and more.

Types of ICICI Bank FD Schemes

Regular Fixed Deposit

How to invest in ICICI FD?

An ICICI Bank FD account can be opened via different modes such as Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Internet Banking

  • Sign in to Internet Banking and open an ICICI Bank FD account via internet banking.
  • Sign in to the NetBanking account and go to the 'Fixed Deposit' option.
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information, and instructions.
  • After successful submission, the FD account will get opened and confirmation will be provided.

2) Mobile App - ICICI Bank App

  • Download the ICICI Bank app and sign in.
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option.
  • Fill out all the required details, nominee information, and instructions.
  • On successful submission, the FD account will get booked.

3) Offline ICICI Bank FD Opening

For opening an ICICI Bank FD account via the offline mode, visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch. An ICICI Bank FD application form must be provided to the bank. Along with this, submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the ICICI Bank.

Tax on ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit

Interest earned on ICICI Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other ICICI Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from ICICI Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a. (Rs. 50,000 p.a. for senior citizens). If the investor fails to provide PAN Card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan against ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit

All Fixed Deposits of ICICI Bank can be withdrawn prematurely except for the tax-saving Fixed Deposit. The bank allows customers to avail of a loan against their ICICI Bank fixed deposit (pledged / secured), subject to the standard authority's discretion. Loan can be availed under ICICI Bank Phone.

Premature Closure of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit

All Fixed Deposits of ICICI Bank can be withdrawn prematurely except for the tax-saving Fixed Deposit. The bank allows customers to withdraw their deposits at any time but with premature withdrawals. The penalty is subject to individual banking norms and is generally in the range of 1% of the interest rate for the period the deposit has remained with the bank.
