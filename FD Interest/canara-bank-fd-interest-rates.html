About Canara Bank

Canara Bank is a renowned name in the banking industry and also one of the largest Nationalized Banks in India. It was established in 1906 and has its headquarters in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Canara Bank provides its customers with a list of fixed deposit options to choose from and has a secure, as part of their savings and investment strategy. An account can be opened for a fixed amount and tenure at attractive interest rates. Along with traditional banking, the savings amount is also insured. Canara Bank fixed deposits get higher interest rates as compared to its savings account.

Features and Benefits of Canara Fixed Deposit

  • The Canara Bank FD interest rates range from 7 days to 10 years.
  • Senior citizens can enjoy an additional interest rate of 0.50% p.a. over regular rates.
  • The minimum deposit required to open an FD with Canara Bank is Rs. 1,000.
  • Nomination facility is provided by the bank.
  • Loan up to 90% of the deposited amount is available.
  • Interest is paid quarterly.
  • Canara Bank provides multiple deposit schemes to its customers, including Regular FD, Tax Saving FD, Senior Citizen FD, and more.

Types of Canara Bank FD Schemes

Regular Fixed Deposit Scheme

  • Available for all customers
  • Tenure from 7 days to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit of ₹1,000
  • Interest rates up to 6.70%
  • Quarterly interest payout option
  • Loan facility available up to 90% of deposit amount

Canara Tax Saver Scheme

  • Lock-in period of 5 years
  • Eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C
  • Maximum investment limit of ₹1.5 lakhs per year
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest rates as per regular FD
  • Quarterly compounding of interest

Canara Bank Kamadhenu Deposit (Reinvestment)

  • Interest compounded quarterly and paid on maturity
  • Minimum deposit of ₹1,000
  • Tenure from 6 months to 10 years
  • Premature closure allowed with penalty
  • Loan/overdraft facility available

Canara Bank Ashraya Deposit Scheme (for Senior Citizens)

  • Available for senior citizens above 60 years
  • Additional interest rate benefit
  • Flexible tenure and payout options
  • Loan/overdraft facility available

How to Invest In Canara Bank FD

A Canara Bank FD account can be opened through different modes like Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Internet Banking

Follow the below-mentioned steps for opening a Canara Bank FD account via internet banking-

  • Sign in to the Canara Bank NetBanking account
  • Navigate to the Fixed Deposit section
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information and furnish the maturity instructions
  • On successful submission of all the information, the FD account will get booked

2) Mobile App - Canara Bank App

Follow the steps mentioned ahead to open an FD account via Canara Bank App-

  • Download the Canara Bank App and sign in
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option
  • Fill out all the required details, nominee information, and provide maturity instructions
  • On successful submission of all the information, the FD account will get booked

3) Offline Canara Bank FD Opening

For opening a Canara Bank FD account via offline mode, one has to visit the nearest Canara Bank branch. Fill out the required Canara Bank FD application form and submit all the necessary documents. Next, deposit the required funds to open the fixed deposit. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the Canara Bank.

Eligibility to Open a Canara Bank FD Account

Those who are looking to invest in Canara Bank fixed deposit schemes should match the following eligibility factors.

  • Resident individuals or NRIs.
  • Individuals looking to make joint accounts or single FD account.
  • Minors under guardianship can also open a fixed deposit account.
  • Companies, organizations and other institutions can also hold an FD account for a Canara Bank FD scheme.

Tax on Canara Bank Fixed Deposit

An investment in Canara Bank Tax Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other Canara Bank FDs entitle to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the Canara Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from Canara Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a.

For senior citizens, the limit is Rs. 50,000 p.a. Also, if the investor fails to provide PAN card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against Canara Bank FD

Canara Bank provides loans against their Fixed Deposits. Investors can avail up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan against Canara Bank Fixed Deposit. The rate of interest will be between 1-2% higher than the FD rate. However, for senior citizens, the differential rate is 1% above the FD rate.

Premature Closure of Canara Bank Fixed Deposit

All Canara Bank Fixed Deposits can be withdrawn prematurely except for the tax-saving Fixed Deposit. Canara Bank applies a penalty for premature withdrawals. The penalty is subject to individual banking norms the same as other banks or as decided by Canara Bank.
