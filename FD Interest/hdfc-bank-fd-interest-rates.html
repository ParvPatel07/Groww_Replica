Features and Benefits of HDFC Fixed Deposit

  • Senior citizens get an additional interest rate of 0.50% p.a. above regular rates.
  • The minimum deposit required to open an FD with HDFC Bank is Rs. 5000.
  • Nomination facility is provided by the bank.
  • Loan up to 90% of the deposited amount is available.
  • Interest is paid quarterly.
  • HDFC Bank provides multiple deposit schemes to its customers, including Regular FD, Tax Saving FD, Senior Citizen FD, and more.

Types of HDFC Bank FD Schemes

Regular Fixed Deposit

  • Available for all customers
  • Tenure from 7 days to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit of ₹5,000
  • Interest rates up to 7.00%
  • Quarterly interest payout option
  • Loan facility available up to 90% of deposit amount
  • Nomination facility available
  • Senior citizens get additional interest

5-Year Tax Saving Fixed Deposit

  • Lock-in period of 5 years
  • Eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C
  • Maximum investment limit of ₹1.5 lakhs per year
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest rates as per regular FD
  • Quarterly compounding of interest

HDFC Foreign Currency Non-Resident (FCNR) Fixed Deposit

  • FCNR (B) FDs allow NRIs to earn interest in foreign currency
  • Tenure from 1 year to 5 years
  • Interest paid half-yearly or on maturity
  • Premature withdrawal allowed (with penalty)
  • Loan/overdraft facility available against FCNR deposit with eligible margin

Green Deposits

  • Invest in HDFC Bank Green Deposit, which is used for eco-friendly, sustainable, and green projects
  • Available for individuals, HUFs, companies, trusts, and societies
  • Tenure from 1 year to 10 years
  • Interest rates as per regular FD

Overdraft Against Fixed Deposits

  • Facility to avail overdraft against FD at concessional lending rate
  • Available for both individuals and non-individuals holding regular or tax-saving FDs
  • Loan/overdraft up to 90% of the deposit amount
  • Interest charged only on the amount utilized

Term wise HDFC Fixed Deposit Rates

Short-term Deposits

  • Tenure ranging from 7 days to 1 year. It does not have a lock-in period, and short-term FDs are offered at 3.00% to 6.00% p.a. for both regular and senior citizens.

Medium-term Deposits

  • Tenure ranging from above 1 year to below 5 years, and offered at 7.00% p.a. for both regular and senior citizens.

Long-term Deposits

  • These deposits have deposit tenure ranging from 5 years to 10 years and offer a rate of 7.00%.

How to invest in HDFC FD?

An HDFC Bank FD account can be opened through different modes like Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Internet Banking

  • Login to NetBanking account.
  • Navigate to the 'Fixed Deposit' option.
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information, and furnish maturity instructions. Click on 'Submit'.
  • On successful submission, the FD account will get booked.

2) Mobile App - HDFC Bank App

  • Download the HDFC Bank Mobile App and sign in.
  • Click on the 'Fixed Deposit' option.
  • Fill out all the required details, nominee information, and furnish the Cheque/ signature option as needed.
  • On successful submission, the FD account will get booked.

3) Opening HDFC FD via Offline

For opening an HDFC Bank FD account via the offline mode, visit the nearest HDFC Bank branch. Fill out the HDFC Bank FD application form and submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the HDFC Bank.

Tax on HDFC Bank Fixed Deposit

HDFC Bank Fixed Deposit is a tax saving investment option entitled to tax deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act. The interest income from the deposit is taxed as per the investor's applicable slab rate. TDS is deducted at 10% if the interest income exceeds Rs. 40,000 (Rs. 50,000 for senior citizens). If PAN is not provided, TDS is deducted at 20%.

Loan against HDFC Bank Fixed Deposits

A fixed deposit holder is eligible for a loan against HDFC FD. The bank approves a loan of up to 90% of the FD's value at a rate of interest 1-2% above the FD rate. Senior citizens get a higher loan limit and lower interest rate. This facility is not available for tax-saving FDs. Loan against FD is available for both individuals and non-individuals, including Regular Fixed Deposit schemes and not tax-saving FDs.

Premature Closure of HDFC Bank Fixed Deposit

HDFC Bank allows premature withdrawal of FDs with applicable fee/penalty clauses. Note that this will be on the lower amount of the interest rate and the period of the deposit.

For tax-saving FDs, premature withdrawal is not allowed. For other FDs, the penalty is 1% on the interest rate for the period the deposit has remained with the bank. The penalty is not applicable for deposits below Rs. 5 lakh if kept for more than 12 months. The penalty is only applicable for deposits above Rs. 5 lakh or for deposits kept for less than 12 months. The premature withdrawal penalty will apply for early breaking of deposits, as per HDFC's policy.
