Features and Benefits of IndusInd FD

  • IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposit is a special savings instrument that helps individuals to generate income. Its most prominent features include:
  • Flexible tenures ranging from 7 days to 3 years
  • Quarterly compounding of interest
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal facility (with penalty)
  • Nomination facility available
  • Special rates for senior citizens

Types of IndusInd Bank FD Schemes

IndusInd Bank Regular FD Scheme

  • Standard deposit scheme for all customers, tenure up to 3 years
  • Minimum deposit: ₹1,000
  • Loan/overdraft facility available
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)
  • Special rates for senior citizens

IndusInd Bank Sweep In/Out scheme

  • FD linked to savings/current account, funds are automatically swept in/out as per threshold amount
  • Tenure: 1 year to 3 years
  • Premature withdrawal permitted (penalty may apply)

India Tax Saver Scheme

  • 5-year lock-in period, eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C
  • Maximum investment: ₹1.5 lakh per annum
  • No premature withdrawal allowed
  • Interest paid quarterly or on maturity

Term-wise IndusInd Bank FD Rates

Short-term Deposits

Short-term IndusInd Bank FDs cover tenures ranging from 7 days to 12 months. IndusInd Bank FDs offered around 3.50% to 6.00%. These deposits are best for investors looking for lower tenure FDs and liquidity.

Medium-term Deposits

Long-term Deposits

How to Open FD in IndusInd Bank?

An IndusInd Bank FD account can be opened through different modes like Mobile App, Internet Banking, and offline.

1) Open FD Account Online on IndusInd Bank App

  • Under IndusInd Bank, choose the "Fixed Deposit" option.
  • Complete the application form, and click on Submit. Post approval, the amount will get transferred from the savings account. The digital receipt, and other information will be sent to the registered email address. A physical slip will also be sent to the registered address.

2) Internet Banking

  • Follow the standard steps for opening an IndusInd Bank FD account via internet banking:
  • Sign in to IndusInd Bank NetBanking account
  • Navigate to the Fixed Deposit section
  • Fill out all the necessary details, nominee information, and maturity instructions
  • On successful submission of all the information, the FD account will get booked. An acknowledgement slip will be available for download and record.

3) Opening IndusInd Bank FD Offline

For opening an IndusInd Bank FD account via offline mode, one has to visit the nearest IndusInd Bank branch. Fill out the required IndusInd Bank FD application form and submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful processing of the application, an FD receipt will be provided by the IndusInd Bank.

Eligibility for IndusInd Bank FD Account

Individuals or entities satisfying the following criteria can opt for a fixed deposit in IndusInd Bank FD –

  • Individuals, including guardians on behalf of minors
  • Partnership firms
  • Limited companies
  • Sole proprietorships
  • HUFs, clubs, associations, trusts, and societies

Documents to Open IndusInd Bank FD

The following documents have to be furnished for opening an IndusInd Bank FD–

  • Identity proof - any one (Aadhaar Card, Passport, PAN card, Driving license, government & senior citizen ID)
  • Address proof - any (Passport, telephone/bill, bank statement with cheque, Aadhaar card, electricity bill)

Tax on IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposits

An investment in IndusInd Bank Saving FD entitles to tax saving up to Rs. 1,50,000 p.a. No other IndusInd Bank FD entitles to tax saving under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Also, note that the interest income from the IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposit is taxable according to the slab rates of income tax. Also, the interest income is subject to a TDS of 10% if the income from IndusInd Bank FD exceeds Rs. 40,000 p.a.

For senior citizens, the limit is Rs. 50,000 p.a. Also, if the investor fails to provide PAN card details to the bank, the TDS rate will be 20%.

Loan Against IndusInd Bank FD

IndusInd Bank provides loans against their Fixed Deposits. Investors can receive up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan. The interest rate will be between 2.0% - 2.5% above the FD rate.

Premature Closure of IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposit

All IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposits can be withdrawn prematurely, excluding the tax-saving Fixed Deposits. No penalty is levied for deposits below Rs. 5 lakhs if kept for 12 months. For fixed deposits greater than Rs. 5 lakhs or without that period, the penalty is enforced on the premature closure of interest.
