Types of Equitas Bank FD Schemes

Equitas Regular Fixed Deposit

  • Available for all customers to invest in tenure ranging between 7 days and 10 years.
  • Minimum deposit: Rs. 5000
  • Premature withdrawal allowed after 6 months and no penalty if withdrawn after 6 months
  • Auto-renewal and nomination facility available

Equitas Selfie FD

  • Special FD currently available only through app and netbanking
  • Minimum deposit: Rs. 5000
  • Tenure: 1 year, 2 years, and 3 years
  • Premature withdrawal allowed after 6 months and no penalty if withdrawn after 6 months
  • Auto-renewal and nomination facility available

Term wise Equitas Fixed Deposit Rates

How to Invest in Equitas FD?

An Equitas Small Finance Bank FD account can be opened through online and offline modes -

1) Online Method

  • Visit Equitas Small Finance Bank's official website.
  • Login using credentials, then choose Personal option, then Deposit, and finally Fixed Deposit.
  • Fill all details, select the required FD type, and click on 'Submit'.
  • Make payment. Your FD will get digitally click on 'Submit'.
  • After successful booking, you will get a confirmation message for future processing of booking this FD.

2) Offline Method

For opening an Equitas Small Finance Bank FD account via offline mode, one has to visit the nearest Equitas Small Finance Bank branch. Fill out the Equitas Small Finance Bank FD Application form and submit all the necessary documents with the deposit amount. On successful verification of the application, an FD will be opened.

Tax on Equitas Small Finance Bank Fixed Deposit

The implications on Equitas Small Finance Bank Fixed Deposit includes - Your TDS will be deducted at a 20% rate as against 10% under section 206AA. Also, no TDS credit or TDS certificate will be provided by the Income Tax Department. Also, note that Form 15G/H and other exemption certificates will remain invoked and penal TDS will be applicable.

Loan Against Equitas Small Finance Bank FD

Equitas Small Finance Bank offers loans up to 90-95% of the FD amount. The interest rate of such loans is usually fixed at 2% higher than the FD interest rate.

Premature Closure of Equitas Small Finance Bank Fixed Deposit

Equitas Small Finance Bank allows premature withdrawal from FD. On withdrawal, an interest is levied by the officials once the booking date and the FD deposit. The penalty will apply only to those FDs with a tenure of more than 7 days. If the tenure is less than 7 days, such FD cannot be prematurely closed.
