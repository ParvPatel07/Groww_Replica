BSE Holidays 2025

Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) is one of the premier stock exchanges operating in India, listing more than 5000 companies. As of May 2024, BSE is the world's 9th-largest stock exchange, with a market capitalisation exceeding US$3.0 trillion on May 24, 2024.

BSE plays an important role in regulating and managing the financial markets of India, channeling idle resources towards productive investments. It provides a platform for trading a wide range of securities, including equities, debt instruments, derivatives, mutual funds, and other sophisticated financial products. With its advanced infrastructure and regulatory oversight, BSE plays a pivotal role in driving economic growth and fostering investor confidence.

BSE Market Timings 2025

BSE trades occur from 9:15 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. on weekdays, whilst Saturday-Sunday is observed as BSE holidays. Pre-market open trading happens between 9:00 to 9:15 a.m. Apart from the weekends, several national holidays falling through the year will affect the share market trading, as mentioned in the table above. The BSE holidays for the year 2025 have also been segregated by the BSE. Trading holidays constitute no transactions in the Bombay Stock Exchange.

On days of clearing BSE holidays, on the other hand, the purchase and sale of securities take place, but financial transfers of resources from investors to companies are postponed as banks remain non-functional on these days.

  • Pre-Open Timings: Modification/order entry begins at 9:00 a.m. and ends at 9:15 a.m. on all business days.
  • Normal Trading Timings: Restricted physical market or normal market begins at 9:15 a.m. and ends at 3:30 p.m. on all business days.
  • Timings of Block Deal Session: The morning block deal window begins at 8:45 a.m. and ends at 9:00 a.m. The afternoon block deal window begins at 2:05 p.m. and ends at 2:20 p.m. The trading session is closed between 3:40 p.m. and 4:00 p.m.

Also, if it is necessary, the trading hours can be expanded, decreased, or advanced as well.
