Link Aadhaar Card to Bank Account

Account holders can link Aadhaar Number with Bank Account online and offline. Aadhaar link to bank account can be done via internet banking, mobile banking app or by personal branch banking. However, bank integration with the Aadhaar database is mandatory. This enables you to access government benefits, transfer multiple government benefits to the same account, and also enables a smooth and secure Aadhaar banking link.

Link Aadhaar Number with Bank Account through Online

Below are the different ways to link Aadhaar number with bank account online. They are through:

  • Internet Banking
  • Mobile Application

Aadhaar Link to Bank Account through Internet Banking

To link your Aadhaar card with your bank account, you can do so using internet banking. The steps are as follows:

  1. Login to your internet banking account.
  2. Go to the "Services" or "Requests" section.
  3. Select the option "Update Aadhaar with Bank Account/DBT" (or you may see "My Account" section, "Aadhaar Registration", etc.).
  4. Register, update, and enter your Aadhaar number and details.
  5. Agree and submit. You will get a confirmation from your bank after successful update.

How to do Aadhaar Banking Linking through Mobile App?

Many banks provide the option for Aadhaar link to bank account via the mobile banking app. The following are the steps that you generally take:

  1. Go to the respective mobile banking app from the Google Play Store or Apple Store.
  2. Open app—go to settings or confirmation for your account (username and password, if there is any security question).
  3. Navigate to "Aadhaar Services" or any similar section.
  4. Look for options that read "Link Aadhaar", "Link Aadhaar/Pradhan Aadhaar Number" or anything similar.
  5. Enter your Aadhaar number and verify with OTP or any other details.
  6. Check your Aadhaar link status (some may be additional or extra for confirmation).
  7. Choose "Update" or "Confirm" and complete the confirmation.

Aadhaar Link with Bank Account through Branch

Follow the steps to get linked with banks via Aadhaar Centres/Bank Accounts through Branch. Used as an offline mode –

  1. Pay a regular visit to your branch.
  2. Carry your Aadhaar card and bank account form.
  3. Fill out the Aadhaar link with bank account form.
  4. Attach a self-attested copy of your Aadhaar card with bank account form.
  5. Submit the form and documents to the bank official. Bank will verify and link Aadhaar with bank account form.
  6. Once the process is complete, you will receive an SMS or email from your bank about successful update.

Aadhaar Card Link with Bank Account through ATM

To link your Aadhaar to your bank account at ATM, typically follow these steps:

  1. Insert the bank debit/credit card into your bank account ATM, select your PIN.
  2. Go to the "Services", "Requests", or "Registration" and select "Aadhaar registration" to link your Aadhaar number with your bank account.
  3. Please check your 12-digit Aadhaar number (you may need to enter this for confirmation).

How to Check Aadhaar Bank Link Status?

You can easily verify whether you have linked bank account to Aadhaar by accessing UIDAI's website, using your Aadhaar number, or by using the bank's mobile app or net banking.

Check Aadhaar Card Link to Bank Account Status through Online

  1. Go to the UIDAI website (https://resident.uidai.gov.in/bank-mapper) and select "Check Aadhaar/Bank Linking Status".
  2. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and security captcha, then press submit. If your Aadhaar is already seeded, you will be securely added to the bank account.
  3. If your registered mobile number is valid, an OTP will be sent to the OTP box or the given code and click the "Submit" button.
  4. The result will display the bank account connected with Aadhaar.

Check Aadhaar Card and Bank Account Linking Status through Mobile

You can also use the USSD codes from your mobile phone number to verify the status of your Aadhaar card and bank account link. This service is only available if your mobile number is linked to your Aadhaar details in the UIDAI database.

  1. Call *99*99# using your UIDAI-registered mobile number.
  2. Then enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number.
  3. The status of your bank account will be displayed if it is linked to your Aadhaar. If no status info displayed, your bank account may possibly not be linked or updated in the UIDAI database.

Check Aadhaar Banking Link Status via Aadhaar

You can also check the current status of your Aadhaar bank account linking via the mAadhaar app by completing with the application's instructions:

  1. Login to the mAadhaar app.
  2. Choose "Aadhaar Bank A/c Link Status" from the My Aadhaar menu.
  3. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and security captcha, and click the "Request OTP" option.
  4. Input the OTP received on your registered mobile, and you will be able to check your Aadhaar Quick Link/Bank linking confirmation.

Benefits of Linking Aadhaar Card and Bank Account

The following are the advantages of connecting Aadhaar numbers to bank accounts:

  • Aadhaar Card is a legal document. One can also be recognized as a legal holder for Know Your Customer (KYC) document, including direct subsidies such as LPG, kerosene, ration, etc.
  • Direct government credit to subsidies and NREGA wages, pensions, scholarships, pensions, and other benefits.
  • The transaction becomes 24x7 digital, including, helping multiple transparency and accountability.
  • The Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS) inter-operable design allows you to access the bank account anytime and from anywhere.
