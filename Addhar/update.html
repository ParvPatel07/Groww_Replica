Aadhar Card Update and Correction

Aadhaar number is a 12-digit unique identity number provided by the UIDAI (Unique Identification of India Authority) to residents of India. It is mandatory for various purposes. Anyone can update their Aadhaar card details, such as name, address, mobile number, date of birth, gender, and biometrics, by visiting the official UIDAI website or Aadhaar Seva Kendra.

This page provides information on how to update Aadhaar card details, required documents, update methods, and other useful guidelines and information. Aadhaar update is possible via both online and offline methods.

How to Update Aadhaar Card?

To change Aadhaar information, two main options are available:

  1. Log in online to update Aadhaar details through UIDAI portal.
  2. Visit an authorized Aadhaar Seva Kendra for offline update.

Aadhaar Card Update Through UIDAI

To update Aadhaar card details online via the Aadhaar self-service update portal, follow the steps given below:

  1. Visit the UIDAI Aadhaar update portal.
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number and captcha, then click "Send OTP" to receive a TOTP/OTP on your registered mobile number.
  3. Enter the OTP and log in to your Aadhaar account. You can also use the TOTP feature as an alternative to SMS-based OTP.
  4. Choose the "Update Address via Address Proof" or "Update Address via Secret Code" as the preferred update option.
  5. Fill in the details, upload scanned copies of the required documents, and submit the request.
  6. Save the URN (Update Request Number) for future reference.

The update request will get accepted, and a URN will be generated.

How to do Aadhaar Card Update Through Aadhaar Seva Kendra?

  1. Visit the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra. A list can be found here: Aadhaar Seva Kendra Locator.
  2. Fill in the Aadhaar update/correction form and provide supporting documents for the update.
  3. Submit the form and required documents to the operator.
  4. Biometric authentication will be done for verification.
  5. Pay the applicable fee for the update.
  6. Collect the acknowledgement slip with the Update Request Number (URN).

How to Update Aadhaar Mobile Number?

  1. Visit the nearest Aadhaar Enrolment/Update Centre.
  2. Fill in the Aadhaar Update Form.
  3. Submit the form and provide your biometrics.
  4. Pay the applicable fee.
  5. Collect the acknowledgement slip with the URN.

Documents Required for Aadhaar Card Update

The Aadhaar updating documents are well-categorized. Aadhaar support documents for address, address proof, date of birth, and relationship. The list of updated documents for the Aadhaar card update is mentioned below.

Aadhaar Support Documents for Proof of Identity

Any of these following documents serve as evidence of identity for Aadhaar card enrollment or update:

  • Passport
  • PAN card
  • Voter's card
  • Ration card
  • Driving license
  • Government photo ID cards

  • Photo bank ATM card
  • ECGC/Kisan photo card
  • Identity certificate

  • Photo credit card

  • Marriage proof document with photo
  • Address card containing name and photo
  • Pensioner photo card
  • Freedom fighter photo card
  • Address card containing name/signature issued by the Department of Posts
