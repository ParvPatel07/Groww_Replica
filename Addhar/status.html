How to Check Aadhar Card Status

According to Government of India, the Aadhaar number is the most significant form of identification for residents. It contains key documents such as photo, signature, address, and other information. However, Aadhaar card status is important for many everyday operations, such as opening a bank account, filing an income tax return, applying for a passport, or availing benefits from government schemes.

Aadhaar status may be updated via Enrolment Centres, online, bank, and post offices. If you have previously applied for data update or fresh Aadhaar, you may check its status (UIDAI official database).

Understand Methods to Check Aadhaar Card Status

Here are multiple methods to process or track Aadhaar card status in detail:

Check Aadhaar Status Online With Enrolment no.

The Aadhaar card status check is simple, and the applicant may check the status of their Aadhaar card online by visiting the official UIDAI website. Follow the steps to check your Aadhaar card status online:

  1. Go to the UIDAI Aadhaar status page.
  2. Enter the details of your Aadhaar card application online, following the procedures detailed below.
  3. Step 1: Visit the UIDAI portal.
  4. Step 2: To check the status of your Aadhaar, you will now require your EID (Enrolment ID).
  5. Step 3: Enter your EID, which is a 14-digit enrolment number and a 14-digit date and time of your enrolment, mentioned on your enrolment acknowledgement slip/acknowledgement page (slip/printout).
  6. Step 4: Enter the Captcha code.
  7. Step 5: Click the "Check Status" button.
  8. Step 6: The UIDAI site will then download Aadhaar updates or Aadhaar.
  9. Step 7: Status of Aadhaar is available to you. Mobile option is valid to get your Aadhaar on mobile.

Note: The status can also be checked via a registered mobile phone.

Aadhaar Status Check without Enrolment Number

If you have forgotten your enrolment number or misplaced your acknowledgement slip, you can locate it and then check the status of your Aadhaar card. Here's how to verify the status of your Aadhaar card without an enrolment number:

  1. Go to the UIDAI retrieve EID/UID page.
  2. Step 1: Input your name, email address, and phone number, usually to obtain a new OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
  3. Step 2: Enter the OTP, and then choose the "Verify OTP" option.
  4. Step 3: You will receive your Enrolment Acknowledgement details on your registered mobile phone.
  5. Step 4: The applicant may verify status of Aadhaar using this enrolment number.

Search Aadhaar Card Status through Name

When you apply for Aadhaar, you can track the status of your application by specifying your Enrolment ID/Name. This enrolment status helps you to check the progress of your Aadhaar card application.

When you go to the respective application or Aadhaar status page, you will update on getting an enrolment number upon successfully submitting your application. You can then check the status using your name and other details.

Receive Aadhaar Card Status by SMS

  1. Send an SMS in the format: UID STATUS <14 digit Enrolment Number> to 51969/1947.
  2. After sending this SMS, UIDAI will update you on the status of your Aadhaar card.

Get Aadhaar Card Status by Post

Once you have updated your Aadhaar, your updated Aadhaar will be mailed by UIDAI, at the mobile or the applicant's address available with UIDAI.

You can also directly enquire at your local post office about status. Post office tracking is possible for quick delivery.

Get your enrolment information, which you may obtain as status on the Aadhaar website.

How to Check Aadhaar Update Status?

There are many ways to check the update status of your Aadhaar card, and they are:

a) Check the Aadhaar Card Update Status through the Aadhaar Number

If you have Aadhaar or an update application URN/SRN/Enrolment ID, you can track the update of Aadhaar number.

  1. Step 1: Go to https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/.
  2. Step 2: Enter the 12-digit number from your UID card in the field given.
  3. Step 3: Do the Captcha verification.

Step 4: The status result will update live or not that modification was made in this database.

b) Check Aadhaar Card Update Status through SRN

You would be given a Service Request Number (SRN) for your in-progressness or Aadhaar Validation Letters. The procedure remains the same as URN, with the exception that you will enter the 14-digit SRN together with the Captcha verification to get the update status.

c) Check the Aadhaar Card Update Status through URN

URN stands for Update Request Number, and it is a 14-digit number generated once you complete the online address update process. When you submit the process, the number will appear on the screen. You will also receive an SMS containing the URN to the applicant's number you have linked to.

This number will also be in the form: 1234/12345/12345.

  1. Step 1: Go to the Aadhaar status page: https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/ and enter the URN.
  2. Step 2: Enter the right Captcha.

d) Check Update Status through Phone

If you don't have internet or PC, you can verify the progress of your updates by calling UIDAI's number (toll-free), 1947. When you dial the number, you will be connected to the IVR. You will have to follow the steps as below to access your status through phone:

  1. Step 1: Language of preference. You need to choose one of the following options: 1 for Hindi, 2 for English, 3 for Kannada, 4 for Malayalam, 5 for Tamil, 6 for Telugu, 7 for Assamese, 8 for Bengali, 9 for Marathi, 10 for Punjabi, 11 for Oriya, 12 for Urdu, 13 for Gujarati.
  2. Step 2: Confirm whether or not you have registered your Aadhaar.
  3. Step 3: If you have registered Aadhaar, press 1. If not, if you haven't yet registered, press 2. Press 2 to check this status.
  4. Step 4: Enquire on update status. Next choose 1 for status of Aadhaar update, 2 for complaint. Press 1 for quick information on the URN. Enter the URN number for status. If you have not received the URN, press 2 to check the status of Aadhaar update by name, date, and time.
  5. Step 5: The IVR automatic commander reveals the result of electronic goods; if you have URN, press 1, else, press 2 for other status.

Step 6: Team assistance/commander reveals result of electronic goods; if you have URN, press 1, else, press 2 for other status.

e) Check Aadhaar Update Status by Post

Once the update is done, you can verify the update status by post. You will receive the update status notification at your address as per the Aadhaar database. You may also visit the local post office for update status information or assistance.
