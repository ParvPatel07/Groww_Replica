mAadhaar App - Download, Register & Password Reset

The mAadhaar app is the official Aadhaar mobile app, developed by UIDAI, to give platform for Aadhaar holders to carry their Aadhaar information digitally. It allows users to manage their Aadhaar profile, download Aadhaar, update details, and use the profile to share demographic and biometric information for various services. This app supports up to 5 Aadhaar family profiles. The app is available on Android and iOS platforms. The mAadhaar app is secure, and the data is kept safe. The mAadhaar app is free to use and can be downloaded from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Process of mAadhaar App Download

  1. Go to the app store (Google Play Store/Apple App Store).
  2. Search for "mAadhaar" and download the app.
  3. Install the app and open it.
  4. Click on "Register My Aadhaar". Select the "resident" option.
  5. Enter your Aadhaar number and captcha.
  6. Create a 4-digit password to protect your app. This password will be required each time the mAadhaar app is logged in to ensure the safety of Aadhaar and associated data.

How to Register your Profile in the mAadhaar App?

To create and add your profile in the mAadhaar app, it is important to scan and enter your profile details correctly. You can add up to 5 Aadhaar profiles of your family members that are also saved on your smartphone. To build your profile, use the steps given below:

  1. Click on "Register My Aadhaar".
  2. Enter the 4-digit password to set up the mAadhaar account.
  3. Click on the "Add profile" button.
  4. Enter the Aadhaar number and captcha.
  5. Enter the OTP, which is sent to the mobile number registered with Aadhaar.
  6. Once the OTP is validated, click on "Verify" to register your profile. You can add up to 5 profiles in the mAadhaar app.

How to Reset mAadhaar Password?

If users want to change their mAadhaar password, they can reset the mAadhaar password by following the steps below:

  1. Open the mAadhaar app.
  2. On the home screen, click the "Menu" option.
  3. Select "Settings" to access the app settings.
  4. Within the settings section, click on "Reset Password".
  5. Enter your current password for verification purposes.
  6. Once verified, you will be directed to a page where you can set a new password.
  7. Enter your new password, followed by the new password again.
  8. Click the "Update Password" button to update Password. The mAadhaar password reset process is now complete.

How to Delete Your Profile from the mAadhaar App?

If you want to delete and remove your profile or wish to have limited mAadhaar number, your profile in the mAadhaar app will be immediately removed. If you are setting up your phone storage, changing your mobile connection, or erasing application data, you will also have to use the password before proceeding to the username reset. Here's what to do if you want to remove your profile:

  1. Launch the mAadhaar app.
  2. Go to Settings, enter your password.
  3. Go to the profile page by clicking on it.
  4. Click the 3-dot menu button in the upper right corner of the program.
  5. Click the "Delete Profile" button.
  6. You will be prompted to enter your 4-digit password.
  7. Once you have entered and verified, you want to remove the profile.
  8. Click the delete button.

Your mAadhaar app profile will be deleted.
