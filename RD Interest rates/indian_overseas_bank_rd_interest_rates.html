Indian Overseas Bank Recurring Deposit (RD) Interest Rates

IOB or Indian Overseas Bank, provides a recurring deposit scheme to its customers with attractive interest rates. An IOB recurring deposit account can be opened for tenure ranging from 6 months to 120 months (10 years). The minimum deposit amount for an IOB RD is Rs. 50. The interest rates offered by the bank vary based on the deposit tenure. Senior citizens are eligible for an additional interest rate of 0.50% on their RD deposits.

Features of IOB Bank RD Interest Rates

  • IOB provides a loan facility up to 90% of the RD amount.
  • The highest IOB RD interest rate offered for general citizens is 6.80% whereas, for senior citizens, it is 7.30%.
  • The minimum deposit accepted by Indian Overseas Bank starts from as low as Rs. 50.
  • A nomination facility is provided by the bank so that you can nominate your family member or spouse to collect the final maturity amount.
  • The deposit tenure ranges from 6 months to 120 months.
  • The minimum deposit amount can be deposited in the multiples of Rs. 5.
  • Any Indian resident, salaried individual, housewife, small trader, self-employed individuals, children over 10 years can open an RD account with IOB.
  • TDS deductions are subject to the existing income tax rules set at the time of opening an account.

How to Calculate IOB RD Interest?

The interest on the Indian Overseas Bank Recurring Deposit can be calculated with the formula:

A= P x [(1+R/n)^(nt)]

Here,

  • A is the Maturity Amount
  • P is the Recurring Deposit installment
  • n is the number of times the interest is compounded.
  • R is the Rate of Interest
  • t is the Tenure.

For example, Mr. H, who lives in Gujarat decides to open an RD account with IOB with a deposit of Rs 4000 for 3 years. The interest rate he is getting is 6.8% In that case, the total interest amount upon maturity will be Rs. 1,55,840 and his total maturity amount will be Rs 1,55,840.

IOB RD Interest Calculation Online

The RD maturity amount you receive is the sum of all the above-given parameters. Alternatively, one can use the IOB RD calculator available over the internet to calculate the amount earned on the deposited amount. The calculator asks for the details of the above given factors:

  • Deposit Amount: The minimum deposit is the initial amount deposited by a customer in their account. The deposit amount can be as low as Rs. 50 for deposits in urban and rural branches, and Rs. 10 for deposits in semi-urban and metro branches.
  • Deposit Tenure: As mentioned in the previous point, lower interest rates are offered for short tenure RDs and vice-versa.

How to Open an RD Account in Indian Overseas Bank?

An IOB RD account can be opened in two ways:

  1. Offline

By visiting the nearest IOB branch with all the required documents.

  1. Online - Through Net banking

If you are already an Indian Overseas Bank account holder, you can use the net-banking credentials to log in to your account and open an e-RD right away. However, if you are not an IOB account holder, you need to open an account first. Once your account is active, you can use the net banking facility and open an e-RD.

Types of IOB RDs

Indian Overseas Bank extends the following RD options for its customers. The RD details in IOB bank are mentioned below â€“

1. Regular Recurring Deposit:

A regular IOB Recurring Deposit account can be opened for a tenure ranging between 6 and 120 months. Interest is paid every quarter. The minimum deposit amount is Rs. 50. You can start your IOB RD with a minimum deposit of Rs. 50.

2. IOB Fexi Recurring Deposit:

A Flexi RD with IOB can be opened with a minimum monthly deposit amount of Rs. 1,000/month. The maximum deposit one can make per month goes up to Rs. 1 Lakh/month and monthly deposits can range between the maximum/minimum deposit amount. The tenure ranges from 6 months to 10 years. Interest is paid quarterly.

IOB Flexi RD comes with a special feature of no limit to the total number of credits admissible to the maximum limit during the financial year. For example, if the maximum limit under Flexi RD account is Rs. 1 Lakh, one can deposit an amount ranging up to 100 times, i.e. Rs. 1 crore. The tenure ranges from 6 months to 10 years. The interest is paid quarterly.

3. IOB Delight Plus:

This IOB Delight Plus RD can be opened by senior citizens. Interest rates on IOB Delight Plus RD are applicable are 0.75% over the regular rates of interest applicable on term deposits. The interest is paid quarterly. The tenure ranges from 6 months to 10 years. Minimum deposit is Rs. 100.

Premature Withdrawal of Indian Overseas Bank RD Amount

IOB allows premature withdrawal of your RD amount before the maturity date but charges a nominal penalty as per bank norms. However, partial withdrawal is not allowed.

Charges applicable to the foreclosure of an RD account with IOB are as follows:
