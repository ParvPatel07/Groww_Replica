Canara Bank Recurring Deposit (RD) Interest Rates

Canara Bank is one of the leading public sector banks in India, providing a wide range of financial products and services to its customers. The Canara Bank Recurring Deposit scheme is a popular savings option for individuals who want to save a fixed amount regularly and earn interest on their deposits. The scheme offers flexible tenures ranging from 6 months to 10 years and allows customers to deposit a minimum amount of Rs. 50 per month. The interest rates offered on Canara Bank RDs vary based on the deposit tenure and amount. Senior citizens are eligible for an additional interest rate of 0.50% on their RD deposits.

Features of Canara Bank RD

  • Minimum deposit amount of Rs. 50 per month.
  • Flexible tenures ranging from 6 months to 10 years.
  • Interest is compounded quarterly.
  • Nomination facility available.
  • Loan facility available against the deposit amount.
  • Premature withdrawal is allowed with a penalty.
  • Easy account opening process through online and offline modes.

How to Calculate Canara Bank RD Interest

The interest on Canara Bank RD is calculated based on the principal amount, interest rate, and tenure. The interest is compounded quarterly. You can use an online Canara Bank RD calculator to estimate the maturity amount.

Eligibility Criteria for Canara Bank RD

  • Resident Individuals
  • Minors (under the guardianship of parents)
  • Hindu Undivided Families (HUFs)
  • Proprietary and Partnership Firms
  • Registered Societies and Associations

Canara Bank RD Premature Withdrawal

Canara Bank allows premature withdrawal of RD deposits. However, a penalty is applicable. The penalty is usually 1% less than the interest rate applicable for the period the deposit remained with the bank.

Loan Against Canara Bank RD

Canara Bank provides a loan facility against the RD deposit. Customers can avail up to 90% of the deposit amount as a loan. The interest rate on the loan is usually 1% to 2% higher than the RD interest rate.

Taxation on Canara Bank RD

Interest earned on Canara Bank RD is subject to TDS if the interest income exceeds Rs. 40,000 for regular customers and Rs. 50,000 for senior citizens in a financial year. TDS is deducted at the rate of 10% if PAN is furnished, otherwise at 20%. The interest income is also taxable as per the individual's income tax slab rate.
