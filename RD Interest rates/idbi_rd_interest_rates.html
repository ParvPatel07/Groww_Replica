IDBI Bank RD Interest Rates 2025

IDBI Bank offers enticing Recurring Deposit interest rates, and the tenure ranges from 1 - 10 years. Established in India, IDBI, short for Industrial Development Bank of India, is a private sector full service universal bank that provides a wide range of financial products. One can open an IDBI RD account by depositing an amount as low as Rs. 100. The detailed interest rates are given down the page.

Features of IDBI RD Interest Rates

  • IDBI offers a loan facility of 90% against your RD account.
  • A nomination facility is provided so that you can nominate someone to collect your maturity amount.
  • The tenure of the deposit ranges from 6 months to 120 months (10 years).
  • An IDBI RD account can be opened by depositing any monthly amount.
  • IDBI deposit rates depend on the market liquidity and repo rate at the time of opening the RD account.
  • The IDBI RD interest rates go up to 6.20% for general citizens and 6.70% for senior citizens.

How to Calculate the Maturity Amount on IDBI RD?

The formula used to calculate the amount received upon maturity is: A = P(1+r/n)^(nt).

Here:

  • 'A' = final amount received
  • 'r' = interest rate
  • 'P' = principal amount deposited
  • 'n' = number of times interest has been compounded
  • 't' = tenure

Components of the IDBI RD

  • Premature Withdrawal Rules of IDBI RD Account

There will be a nominal amount of penalty on the applicable interest rate according to the RD scheme chosen in case there is a premature withdrawal to withdraw the deposited amount before the maturity date. Also, a partial withdrawal facility is not available at IDBI.

  • IDBI Recurring Deposit Scheme Eligibility

To open an RD account, one must be a citizen of India or a HUF (Hindu Undivided Families).

  • An NRI can also get an IDBI RD account through NRO (Non- Resident Ordinary Account) and NRE (Non-Resident External Account) accounts.
  • A Minor can also get an RD account opened in IDBI given that his/her parents will supervise the finances under their guardianship.

IDBI RD Tax Benefits

IDBI and all other recurring deposits are taxable under the Income Tax Act, 1961. The money you invest in RD is included in your annual income as income Tax (TDS) at Source (TDS) of 10% is applicable on the earned interest amount if it is above the exemption limit of Rs 40,000 per year. The exemption limit is Rs 50,000 for senior citizens. To evade TDS on your RD profit, you can fill either Form 15G or Form 15H.

Penalty on Delayed Installment of IDBI Bank RD account

The bank may provide you with some extra days grace in case you are unable to make the payment beyond the due date due to some reasons. However, frequent irregular payments will not be entertained by the bank and a nominal amount of penalty will be levied by the bank as per store norms.

IDBI Bank RD Interest Rate Deciding Factors

The RD maturity amount including the interest rate depends on the following parameters:

  • Deposit Amount: The total amount deposited by a customer in their account. The deposit amount for IDBI RD starts from as low as Rs.100.
  • IDBI RD Interest Rates: IDBI bank offers a comparatively higher rate of interest on recurring deposits with longer tenure and lower interest rates for an account opened for a shorter period. The highest interest rate offered by the bank is 6.70% for the deposit amount.
  • Deposit Tenure: As mentioned in the previous point, low interest rates are offered for short tenure RDs and vice-versa.

Types of IDBI RD Schemes

  1. SSP - Systematic Savings Plan

IDBI Systematic Savings Plan (SSP) enables them to save at their leisure. Every month, anyone can begin saving any fixed amount ranging from INR 100 to less than INR 2 crore. This strategy is appropriate for pre-planned financial objectives.

  • It is from 1 year to 10 years.
  • The minimum deposit is Rs.100.
  • The maximum deposit is Rs. 2 crores.
  • The RD can have a nominee.
  1. SSP Plus - Systematic Savings Plan Plus

The SSP Plus scheme provides regular savings with principal and interest protection, as well as complimentary insurance coverage and incentive points.

  • It ranges from 2 years to 10 years.
  • The minimum deposit for the plan is Rs.5000 and thereafter Rs.100.
  • The maximum deposit for the plan is Rs. 2 crores.
  • There is a personal accident insurance cover.
  • It has the option to auto-debit.
  • There are no TDS deducted if the depositor submits form 15G or 15H.
  • Premature closure is applicable.

Factors Affecting IDBI Bank Recurring Deposit Rates

RBI's Policies: Certain factors like the change in Cash Reserve Ratios (CRR) and repo rate as set by the RBI may affect the interest rate offered by IDBI bank on Recurring Deposit Accounts.

Recession: RBI releases funds in the market in times of recession, hence surfacing liquidity in the market. Owing to the high liquidity and low credit demand in the market, banks reduce their rates on RD.

Inflation: Inflation, on the other hand, has a positive effect on RD rates, and the market witnesses a rise in interest rates at the time of inflation.

State of the economy in India: With the economy going leaps and bounds, people tend to take larger credits to fulfil their demands. Higher credit demand leads to banks to charge high interest rates on loans, which allows higher interest rates to collect more funds through recurring deposits.

How to Open RD in IDBI Bank Online?

The IDBI RD account can be opened either offline by visiting your nearest IDBI bank branch or logging on to the bank's net banking if you already have an account with the bank.
