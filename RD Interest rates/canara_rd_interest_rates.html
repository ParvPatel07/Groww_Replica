How is the Maturity Amount Calculated on RD Investment?

The maturity amount in the lumpsum amount one gets after the required RD tenure is over. You can determine the maturity amount through the Bank of Baroda RD calculator available on their website that helps you calculating the required interest. however, below given is the formula to calculate the interest on your deposit:

A = P(1+r/n)^(nt)

Where 'A' is the rate of interest, 'P' is the principal deposited, 'r' is the tenure in months and 't' is the applicable interest.

Eligibility Criteria for Canara Bank Recurring Deposits

  • Canara Bank RD account can be operated by any individual, joint account, guardian of a minor, Hindu Undivided Family, Sole proprietorship firm, a company, an association, a trust, or any institution, given they fulfill the eligibility criteria.
  • Non-resident Indians are also eligible to open RD account provided they do it through NRE or NRO accounts.
  • Minors can open an RD account under the supervision of parents or legal guardians.

Considerations of Canara Bank RD 2025

Deposit Amount A Canara Bank RD account can be opened with as low as Rs. 50 for a fixed term without any cap on the maximum amount.
Tenure of deposit An RD account in Canara Bank can be opened for a minimum time period of 6 months to a maximum time period of 10 years.
Rate of Interest Depending on the investment tenure and the account term, Canara Bank compounds the interest on a quarterly basis. However, individuals above 60 years (an additional 0.50% interest rate above the regular rates for individuals below 60 years of age.
Penalty for Delayed Installment Payment Individuals will be charged a penalty of Re. 1 for every Rs 100 payment on every Rs. 100, per month, for all tenures less than or equal to 5 years. For tenures above 5 years, the penalty charged will be Rs. 2 for every Rs. 100 installments.
Premature Withdrawal Facility Canara Bank allows the option of premature withdrawals but it is subjected to a penalty of 1% rate of the account was opened on or after 4th February 2020.
Tax Exemptions Canara Bank recurring deposit is taxable under the Income Tax Act 1961. The money invested in RD is included in your yearly income and a TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) of 10% is applicable on your RD account. In case your income is less than taxable, no tax is TDS on your RD interest profit, you can fill either a form 15G or Form 15H.
Terms to Submit the 15G Application
  • Applicant must be an individual or HUF
  • Only Resident Indians can apply
  • Age should be less than 60 years.
  • Tax calculated on your total income must be zero.
  • The total income from interests or the total income from interests in the previous year should be less than the minimum amount exempted from income tax for that year. Which is, Rs 2.5 lakhs for the 2023-24 (AY 2024-25).

Types of Canara Bank RD Account

  • Regular Recurring Deposit

    This is the regular type RD scheme where one can make a minimum deposit of Rs.500 per month for the selected tenure. Canara Bank also allows you to invest in multiples of Rs.100 for this account i.e to quarterly or six months deposit of three months thereafter. The minimum period is 120 months.

  • Canara Bank Dhanavarsha Recurring Deposit Scheme – Flexi RD Scheme

    One of the best advantages Canara Bank provides is this Canara Dhanavarsha recurring deposit scheme which allows investors to have a flexible deposit amount. With this scheme, investors can deposit any amount on a monthly basis with an option to invest surplus/bonus funds into their Canara Dhanavarsha account. These extra deposits made by the investors earn interest at the contracted interest rate instead over the eighteen months on the regular monthly deposits.

    Canara Bank Dhanavarsha Recurring Deposit Scheme is available to individuals and non-individual investors. The Canara Bank Dhanavarsha RD account can be opened for a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 10 years by making a minimum monthly deposit of Rs.1000 and in multiples of Rs.100. This scheme also provides features like nomination facility, loan facility with an option to avail a loan of up to 90% of the deposited amount. Partial withdrawal facility is not available. Other facilities such as nomination, loan facilities, and others are applicable in this scheme.

  • Nitya Nidhi Deposit Scheme

    This Pigmy Deposit Scheme comes after the merger of Syndicate Bank and Canara Bank. The New Nitya Nidhi Deposit Scheme is a recurring deposit scheme which allows investors to deposit a fixed amount on a daily basis with savings of daily savings and onsite daily door collection through the bank agents. The minimum investment required to avail of this facility is Rs.50 per day and it is also available online or offline mode to applicants at 0.75% p.a.
