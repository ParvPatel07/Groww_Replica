RBL Bank RD Interest Rates

RBL Bank is among the renowned financial institutions of the country with an extensive lineup of financial products and services on offer for a varied range of customer needs. RBL Bank recurring deposit is one of the most sought-after investment options for people who wish to save a fixed amount regularly and earn interest on their deposit to meet their future in terms of finances. The maximum tenure for a RBL Bank RD is 10 years, and the minimum is 6 months. The RBL Bank RD interest rates depend on the amount and the tenure of the deposit. Generally, for a tenure of 6 months to 1 year, the interest rate is 6.00% p.a. or more depending on the amount. In case of senior citizens, the bank provides an extra 0.50% over the regular rate of interest to people who are more than 60 years of age.

Features of RBL Bank RD Interest Rates

  • RBL Bank offers attractive interest with the highest being 6.50% p.a. for general citizens whereas, for senior citizens, the maximum rate goes up to 7.00% p.a.
  • The deposit tenure ranges from 6 months to 240 months (20 years)
  • RBL Bank RD interest is compounded annually
  • RBL provides a nomination facility and the nominee can also collect the final maturity amount
  • Tax is levied as per the Income Tax rules and regulations at the time of opening an account
  • An RBL Bank RD account can be opened either by net banking or by visiting any nearest branch
  • The minimum deposit amount required to open an RBL bank RD account is as low as Rs. 500
  • The minimum tenure is 6 months
  • The penalty is applicable as per the bank norms if a customer misses a monthly payment on the due date

How to Calculate RBL Bank RD Interest?

The interest on the RBL Recurring Deposit is compounded each quarter.

The formula used to calculate the maturity value is -

A= P x [(1+R/n)^(nt)]

Here,

  • A represents the Maturity amount
  • P represents a Recurring Deposit amount deposited monthly
  • n represents the number of times the interest is compounded
  • R represents the Rate of Interest
  • t represents the Tenure

For example, Mr. C, who lives in Ambala opens an RD account with the RBL Bank with a deposit of Rs 1000 for 3 years. The applicable interest rate is 6% p.a. The interest earned on the invested amount at the end of the maturity period will be Rs. 5516 and his total maturity amount will be Rs 35516.

Who is Eligible to Open a Recurring Deposit Account with RBL Bank?

  • Any Indian Resident can open an RD account in RBL Bank either in a single name or jointly with another individual
  • If the applicant is below 18 years of age, he/she should open an RD account under the guardianship of their parents
  • Partnership firms, Institutions, partnership firms, and other organizations including HUF are eligible to open a recurring deposit account
  • Clubs, association, and other non-commercial organizations are also eligible for such account

Penalty on Delayed Installment of RBL Bank RD account

The bank may provide you with some extra time period in case you miss making the payment by the due date due to some reasons. However, frequent irregular payments will not be entertained by the bank and a nominal amount of penalty will be levied by the bank as per their norms.

Premature Withdrawal of RBL Bank RD

RBL usually doesn't allow Premature withdrawal of recurring deposit amounts, but under certain circumstances, it is permissible; however, RBL Bank may charge a penalty according to their current guidelines. In case of late monthly deposits on part of account holders, a penalty on the due amount is levied as per the bank policy, provided the deposit date is after the grace period is over.

Taxation on RBL Bank RD Interest Rates

TDS is deducted as per the Income tax regulations set at the time of opening the account. However, if the interest accrued from the deposit during the financial year exceeds Rs. 10,000 and the PAN details are not provided, the TDS will be deducted at a higher rate, i.e. 20%.

Additional interest on income would attract a tax that would be charged as per an individual's income tax slab.
