Instant Pan Card - How to Download e-PAN Card?

UTIITSL now issues instant e-PAN cards without hassle! You can check the status of your e-PAN card online. It may be downloaded within minutes from the NSDL or the UTIITSL portal. When applying for an e-PAN card, one can get the pan card online in 6 hours and often within 10-15 mins.

e-PAN is also issued by a similar process as regular PAN cards, such as filing e-FIR, uploading a Cancelled card, linking Aadhaar, and downloading as a color card or e-PAN card.

Eligibility for Instant e PAN

  • To apply for e-PAN card, an applicant must meet the following requirements:
  • Individual (Hindu undivided, not corporation, trust, or association), can apply for a new or instant pan card.
  • A valid Aadhaar card is required for all regular applicants.
  • A valid Aadhaar number is required for the application.
  • The mobile phone number for the applicant must be connected to the Aadhaar card.
  • A valid Aadhaar card should be original and valid.

Download Instant PAN Card from NSDL

There are two ways to download an e-PAN from the NSDL portal:

  • Using the Acknowledgement Number
  • Using the PAN Number

Here are the steps to download an e-PAN card from the NSDL portal:

  1. Go to the NSDL ePAN download page: https://www.onlineservices.nsdl.com/paam/MPanLogin.html
  2. Select your "Acknowledgement Number" or "PAN" and fill in the required details as shown in the image above.
  3. Click "Submit" to download your e-PAN card.

How to Download e PAN Card from UTIITSL?

UTIITSL is an online platform to download e-PAN cards, apply for new PAN cards or make changes to existing PAN cards. The steps to download e-PAN are as follows (UTIITSL also allows linking, card reissuance, and online downloads):

  1. Go to the UTIITSL ePAN download page: https://www.pan.utiitsl.com/PAN_ONLINE/ePANCard
  2. Enter your PAN, date of birth/incorporation, GSTIN (optional), captcha, and security code.
  3. Click "Submit" to download your e-PAN card.

Steps to Download Instant PAN Card through Aadhaar Card

One can also download a PAN e-filing solution through an Aadhaar card as shown:

  1. Go to https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/
  2. Select the "Instant e-PAN" option, then click "Check Status / Download PAN".
  3. Enter your Aadhaar number, captcha, and click "Continue". You will be taken to a new page to enter the OTP. Click the Submit button.
  4. After entering the OTP, your authentication is validated, and the e-PAN download page allows you to download your e-PAN card.
