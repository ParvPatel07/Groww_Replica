Pan Card Acknowledgement Number

The PAN card acknowledgement number is given to a person when they apply for a PAN card through NSDL, UTI, or eMudra. A 15-digit acknowledgement number is provided, which helps in tracking the status of the PAN application. NSDL, UTI, and eMudra applicants can check the status of their PAN application using this number. Public can also print/download the PAN card using this acknowledgement number.

This number is vital for status verification, digital download, and for payment. If the applicant's details differ, you can use the acknowledgement number to check the status, download the e-PAN, or re-issue a card. Lost or stolen cards can also be tracked with this number.

How to Find Acknowledgement Number of PAN Card?

Acknowledgement number is provided to applicants at the time of application and is also given in the e-receipt, email/SMS confirmation, and on the application form. Applicants can also log in to the NSDL or UTIITSL portal to retrieve the acknowledgement number if lost. The acknowledgement number is required for tracking the application and for downloading the e-PAN card.

Download Pan Card with Acknowledgement Number

You can download a Pan Card with an NSDL Portal using the acknowledgement number. The steps to download a Pan Card are as follows:

  1. Navigate to the NSDL and then enter the acknowledgement number.
  2. Check Pan Card application status, download e-PAN, and enter VYYN number. Enter the captcha code as shown.
  3. Click the payment option and complete the payment as detailed and click the Generate OTP button.
  4. Enter the OTP you have received on your mobile number.
  5. The e-PAN and its 15-digit acknowledgement number can be used to obtain the Pan Card PDF or e-PAN. The e-PAN Card is provided as a PDF file, which can be downloaded and printed. You can also use the acknowledgement number to check the status of your application.

How to Download Pan Card Acknowledgement Number Online?

As previously stated, the 15-digit acknowledgement number is provided after submitting the PAN application form. This number is required for tracking the status of the application, downloading the e-PAN, and for all future correspondence. If you have lost your acknowledgement number, you can retrieve it by logging in to the NSDL or UTIITSL portal with the details of the application.

How to Track Pan Card Status with Acknowledgement Number?

Individuals who have lost their acknowledgement number can check the status of their PAN card application using the acknowledgement number on the official website. The steps are as follows. This can also be used to check the status of an application for a new or re-issued PAN card.

  1. Visit https://tin.tin.nsdl.com/pantan/StatusTrack.html to get started.
  2. Type in your "Acknowledgement Number".
  3. Type in the Captcha Code.
  4. Press the "Submit" option.

Check PAN Card Status Without Acknowledgement Number

There are a few ways in which you can check your PAN card status without the acknowledgement number:

  1. Through Name and Date of Birth on NSDL Website
    https://tin.tin.nsdl.com/pantan/StatusTrack.html
    Step 1: Provide the name as it appears on your PAN application (First Name, Middle Name, and Last Name/Surname).
    Step 2: Enter date of birth and application acknowledgement (matching to user and the stated formation of a body of individuals or persons) and click the Search button.
  2. Through Coupon Card on UTI Portal
    Visit the UTIITSL website and go to the "Track PAN Card Application Status Page".
    Enter the coupon number and date of birth to see the corresponding solution.
    Enter the captcha code provided and click on Submit.
  3. Through the PAN Number (UTI Portal)
    https://www.pan.utiitsl.com/PAN_ONLINE/trackApp
    Step 1: Enter your PAN number, date of birth, and captcha. If you have a GSTIN, enter it here.
    Step 2: Track the card by clicking submit.
    Step 3: Check email/SMS, where you will receive the status.
    Step 4: Once the OTP and payment process is complete (where payment is due), you can download your e-PAN or check its status online on UTIITSL. (Immediately, you will be able to see the status and download the e-PAN card.)
  4. Through SMS
    UTI Infrastructure Technology Limited Land Line Centre at 020-27218080 or Aaykar Sampark Kendra toll-free number at 1800-180-1961. Applicants can also send an SMS to 57575 with the status as follows: NSDLPAN <15 digit acknowledgement number> to 57575. The status will be sent to your mobile number. For UTIITSL, applicants can send an SMS to 57575 in the format: PAN to 57575.
