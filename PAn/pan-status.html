How to Track PAN Card Status

If you have applied for a new or Reprinting PAN Card but have not heard from the department in a while, you must track its status digitally to ensure there are no delays. You may check your PAN Card status using various methods, including the acknowledgement number, PAN number, date of birth, coupon number, and application number.

Ways to Track Status of PAN Card

Once you submit/receive the application for PAN Card or changes/corrections in information on an existing PAN Card, you may check the status online. You can track PAN status for both the NSDL Portal, UTIITSL, and e-Mudra.

Find several ways to check your PAN Card Status below:

  1. Track UTIITSL PAN Card Status Online
  2. Track PAN Card Status on Protean (formerly NSDL) Website

1) Track PAN Card Status on Protean (NSDL) Website

The status may be checked on the Protean (formerly NSDL) website by entering the application type and acknowledgement number. Here are the steps:

  1. Go to the Protean (NSDL) PAN Card Status page.
  2. On this website, select your Application Type and enter click the status "New/Change Request".
  3. Enter and enter your Acknowledgement Number.
  4. Click to display the status, enter the code.
  5. Click the Submit button.

2) Track PAN Card Status on UTIITSL Website

To track PAN Card status on UTIITSL, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the UTIITSL PAN Card Tracking page.
  2. Enter your PAN number or the Application Coupon Number.
  3. Enter the Captcha (with fields below displayed).
  4. Click the Submit button.
  5. The status of your PAN application will be displayed on the corresponding screen.

3) How to Track PAN Card Status by Name and Date of Birth

There is presently no facility for tracking new PAN Card Status directly by name or application status by Date of Birth. Nevertheless, you may check your PAN Card status by visiting your PAN Policy service or even name and date of birth by following the instructions listed below:

  1. Visit the Tax Information Network of India (access the Department Website's Track your PAN/TAN Application Status page).
  2. Select the Application Type of PAN: "New/Change Request".
  3. Enter information such as your PAN Number, full name, and date of birth.
  4. Click to display your status, enter code, and last name.
  5. Review your PAN details.
  6. To verify the PAN status, click Submit.

4) How to Check PAN Card Status by Coupon Number?

  1. To check the status of your PAN Application visit the PAN Card Application Status window at the UTIITSL website.
  2. Select the Application type from the given drop-down option.
  3. Enter Coupon Number (issued/printed when submitting a PAN application).
  4. Click to check the Submit button.

5) Other Ways to Check PAN Card Status (Telephone, SMS, Without Acknowledgement Number)

Check Status Through Telephone
The applicant must call the TIN call center at 020-27218080 between 7:00 am and 11:00 pm. The applicant must provide the 15-digit acknowledgement number to get the status update.
Check Status Through SMS
By successfully submitting the application and status request, applicants can send an SMS to 57575 with the following format:
NSDLPAN <15 digit acknowledgement number> to 57575. The status will be sent to the applicant's mobile number.
Check Status Without Acknowledgement Number
In case of accidentally misplacing the application, the applicant must make use of the PAN number, name, and date of birth to check the status. Visit the NSDL PAN Card Status page and enter the required details to verify the PAN status and obtain the status of the same.
