Pan Card Verification Online Process

You can verify pan cards online using the following steps detailed below:

  1. Go to the NSDL PAN verification webpage or Income Tax e-filing portal for the NSDL PAN verification process.
  2. Enter your PAN, date of birth, and captcha code, and click "Validate".
  3. Your PAN status, name (as per the PAN), and the status of getting the acknowledgement number.
  4. Your result will show the status online, along with the PAN number verification status.
  5. You may print the verification status or bring a digital soft print using the acknowledgement number.

Online Pan Verification by Pan Number

You can also pay for PAN verification online using the PAN number and follow these steps:

  1. Step 1: Go to the Income Tax e-filing portal: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/
  2. Step 2: Enter your PAN and date of birth, and mobile number for PAN bulk number verification.
  3. Step 3: Click on Continue.
  4. Step 4: Enter the OTP that is sent to your mobile number and click on "Validate".
  5. Step 5: Check the page as the status page appears.

How to Verify PAN Online Under Section 194N?

Tax authorities in India must use their status to verify the pan card online under Section 194N:

  1. Step 1: Go to the Income Tax Portal and log in.
  2. Step 2: Enter your PAN and Aadhaar details and click the "Continue" button.
  3. Step 3: Accept all requests and confirmation by clicking the "Continue" button.
  4. Step 4: The system will confirm the status using its details and return a verification of application under Section 194N.

How to e-verify your Return Using Aadhaar Card?

  1. Step 1: Go to the e-Filing Portal and log in.
  2. Step 2: Log in to your account by providing your PAN, password, and captcha information.
  3. Step 3: Go to the e-Verify Return option.
  4. Step 4: Choose the option "I would like to generate an Aadhaar OTP to e-verify my return" option.
  5. Step 5: Enter the Aadhaar password (OTP) as provided to the registered cellphone.
  6. Step 6: Finish the verification process and submit the OTP.
