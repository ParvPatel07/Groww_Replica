Know Your PAN Card

PAN is a 10-digit identification number assigned to each individual who applies for it, and it also enables to track financial transactions. The PAN is unique, unchangeable, and can be used as an identification card in all financial activities and tax-related operations.

Individuals who forget or misplace their PAN card number can use several official methods to check or download it. These methods are also available for checking PAN status, application status, and other information related to PAN cards.

Know Your PAN Card Number from the Income Tax Website

If you have forgotten your PAN card number, you can retrieve it easily. Visit the official site to log in through the e-filing portal. The process is simple and can be done online. The Department of Filing allows you to check the status and details of your PAN, including the PAN number, by following these steps:

  1. Visit the Income Tax e-filing website.
  2. Click on the "Know Your PAN" option.
  3. Log in using your credentials or register if not already registered.
  4. Complete the Registration Form and submit the "Submit" button.
  5. You will receive an OTP on the email address and cell number you entered.
  6. Enter the OTP to check the e-filing registration procedure.
  7. Go to the e-Filing website and sign in to your account.
  8. Click on "Profile settings."
  9. Click on "PAN details."

Know Your PAN Number by Name and DOB

PAN Cardholders may retrieve their PAN number just by providing their name and date. Therefore, you can know your PAN by name and date by following this method:

  1. Visit the Income Tax e-filing website.
  2. Click on the "Know Your PAN" option.
  3. Enter your PAN details, name, and date of birth, and click "Continue."
  4. Enter the details per your PAN, such as whether you are an individual, Hindu undivided family, an association of persons, a body of individuals, a company, a partnership, a trust, a local authority, an artificial juridical person, or a government agency.
  5. In the next step, enter the "Captcha Code" for verification.
  6. Click the "Submit" button.
  7. Once the procedure is complete, your details, "Your Profile details," with the information matches with the PAN in the database, will be displayed.

Know Your PAN Card Number by Email

You may find out the status of your PAN by sending an email to one or more email addresses: either tininfo@nsdl.co.in (NSDL) or utiitsl.gsd@utiitsl.com (UTIITSL). The email should contain your details.

Know Your PAN Card through Mobile Application

  1. Download the official app that allows you to check your PAN details. You need to enable PAN status for verification, authentication, and other purposes.
  2. Open the app and go to the "Know Your PAN" option.
  3. Enter the required details, such as your name, date of birth, and other details, under the OTP. Click the Submit button.
  4. Once your authentication is successful, you will be redirected to a new page and can view your PAN details.
