PAN Card Customer Care Number

Every individual who links their status with their tax status must have a PAN (Permanent account number) card. This is because it is a status for identification of an individual and is used for the 15-digit alphanumeric PAN number. The Government of India has set up various customer care and complaint numbers to help applicants and cardholders track the status of their PAN card, resolve issues, and get information about PAN services.

Below are the main customer care numbers, support lines, and email contacts for PAN card-related queries, including the NSDL and UTIITSL helplines and feedback platforms.

UTI Pan Card Customer Care Services

UTI Infrastructure Technology Services Limited (UTIITSL) is a government-owned company in India that provides PAN card services. They provide services like instant PAN card issuance, reissuance, and processing (eKYC, eSign, ePAN, etc.).

Applicants can check the status of their PAN, the progress of your PAN registration, PAN verification, PAN verification status, and more through UTIITSL.

UTIITSL PAN Customer Care Number

Customers can register a grievance or make a query online at the UTI Infrastructure Technology Services Limited (UTIITSL) website, in addition to calling or emailing. Below are the main customer care contacts for the NSDL (Income Tax Department) and UTI Infrastructure Technology Services Limited (UTIITSL).

  • Telephone Number: 033-40802999, 033-40802900
  • Email ID: utiitsl.gsd@utiitsl.com

PAN Card Customer Feedback Platform

One can submit feedback or complaints about their PAN card, file a complaint, or even provide feedback by going to the feedback platform. The main feedback platform is Aaykar Sampark Kendra (Design / www.incometax.gov.in):

  • Telephone Number: 1800-180-1961 (toll-free)

Email: ask@incometax.gov.in
