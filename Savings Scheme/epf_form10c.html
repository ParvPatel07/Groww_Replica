EPF Form 10C

EPF Form 10C is used by members of the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) to withdraw their Provident Fund contributions or to obtain an Employees' Pension Scheme (EPS) Scheme Certificate.

What is EPF Form 10C?

EPF Form 10C is an application form for withdrawal of Provident Fund accumulation before completing 10 years of service, or for obtaining an EPS Scheme Certificate. This form is relevant for members who are leaving their employment before becoming eligible for a monthly pension under EPS.

Purpose of EPF Form 10C

The primary purposes of EPF Form 10C are:

  • To withdraw the EPF amount if the service period is less than 10 years.
  • To obtain an EPS Scheme Certificate if the service period is 10 years or more but the member is not yet 58 years old. The Scheme Certificate allows for the transfer of pensionable service to a new employer or for claiming pension later.

Who is Eligible to Apply for EPF Form 10C?

The following categories of members are eligible to use EPF Form 10C:

For Withdrawal of EPF Funds:

  • Members who have not completed 10 years of eligible service.
  • Members who have completed 10 years of service but have not attained the age of 58 and are not opting for a reduced pension.

For Obtaining EPS Scheme Certificate:

  • Members who have completed 10 years of eligible service but have not attained the age of 58 years.
  • Members who are above 50 years but below 58 years and are not willing to take a reduced pension.

Details to be Filled in EPF Form 10C

EPF Form 10C requires the following details to be filled in by the applicant:

  1. Name of the applicant
  2. Date of birth
  3. Father's/Husband's name
  4. Address
  5. EPF Account Number
  6. Date of joining and exit from employment
  7. Bank Account Details (Account Number, Bank Name, IFSC Code)
  8. Reason for leaving service
  9. Option for withdrawal benefit or Scheme Certificate

How to Fill and Submit EPF Form 10C

EPF Form 10C can be filled and submitted both online and offline.

Offline Submission:

Download the EPF Form 10C from the EPFO website. Fill in all the required details accurately. Attach the necessary documents, such as identity proof, address proof, bank passbook/cancelled cheque. Submit the filled form along with the documents to the nearest EPFO office through your employer.

Online Submission:

Log in to the EPFO member portal using your UAN and password. Navigate to the 'Online Services' tab and select 'Claim (Form - 31, 19, & 10C)'. Select the option for withdrawal (Form 10C). Fill in the required details online and upload the scanned copies of the necessary documents. Submit the application online. The claim status can be tracked on the member portal.

Documents Required for EPF Form 10C

The following documents are generally required for submitting EPF Form 10C:

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport)
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License, Utility Bills)
  • Bank Passbook or Cancelled Cheque (showing Account Number and IFSC Code)
  • UAN Card

Importance of EPF Form 10C

EPF Form 10C is important for members who are leaving their job before retirement age. It allows them to access their EPF savings or to ensure their pensionable service is accounted for, providing financial flexibility and security for their future.
