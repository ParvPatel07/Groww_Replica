EPF Form 11

EPF Form 11 is a self-declaration form filled by a new employee joining a company that is covered under the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) Act, 1952. This form serves as an undertaking by the employee to transfer their previous EPF account to the new employer and declare if they are already an EPF member.

What is EPF Form 11?

EPF Form 11 is a declaration form that collects necessary information from a new employee to determine their eligibility for EPF membership and to facilitate the transfer of their existing EPF account (if any). This form is crucial for the employer to enroll the new employee under the EPF scheme and ensure continuity of their provident fund contributions.

Purpose of EPF Form 11

The main purposes of EPF Form 11 are:

  • To collect essential details of a new employee for EPF registration.
  • To obtain the employee's declaration about their previous EPF membership and UAN.
  • To facilitate the online transfer of the previous EPF account to the new employer.
  • To ensure that the employee's provident fund contributions are seamlessly continued.

Who Should Fill EPF Form 11?

Every new employee joining a company that is covered under the EPF Act, 1952, is required to fill and submit EPF Form 11 to their new employer. This form is mandatory even if the employee is not already an EPF member.

Details to be Filled in EPF Form 11

EPF Form 11 requires the following details to be filled in by the employee:

  1. Employee's Name
  2. Father's Name or Husband's Name (for married women)
  3. Date of Birth
  4. Gender
  5. Marital Status
  6. Aadhaar Number
  7. PAN Number
  8. Previous Employment Details (Name and Address of previous employer, EPF Account Number, Date of Joining and Exit)
  9. UAN (if already an EPF member)
  10. Bank Account Details (Account Number, Bank Name, IFSC Code)
  11. Nomination details (similar to Form 2)
  12. Declaration regarding previous EPF membership and willingness to transfer the account.

How to Fill and Submit EPF Form 11

EPF Form 11 is typically provided by the new employer at the time of joining. The employee needs to fill in all the required details accurately and sign the form. The filled form should be submitted to the employer along with copies of supporting documents like Aadhaar card, PAN card, and bank passbook.

The employer will then verify the details and submit the form to the concerned EPFO office for updating the employee's EPF records and initiating the transfer process if applicable.

Documents Required for EPF Form 11

The following documents are generally required for submitting EPF Form 11:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Passbook or Cancelled Cheque
  • UAN Card (if available)

Importance of EPF Form 11

EPF Form 11 is essential for the smooth transition of an employee's provident fund benefits when changing jobs. It ensures that the previous EPF accumulations are transferred to the new account, allowing for continuity of service and compounding of interest. It also helps the employer to comply with EPF regulations and enroll the employee under the scheme correctly.
