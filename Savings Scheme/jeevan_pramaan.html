Jeevan Pramaan

To encourage financial independence after retirement, the Government introduced various sponsored pension schemes. A key initiative is the Jeevan Pramaan, a digital service that allows pensioners to furnish a Life Certificate to the institution that is authorized to disburse pension into their account.

Such a life certificate serves as proof of their existence for receiving a pension. Earlier the process of availing a life certificate involved physically visiting the disbursing authority or getting it attested by an authorized person and submitting. To eliminate the hassles that come with pension disbursement, the government of India digitised the process and launched the Jeevan Pramaan service for pensioners.

What is the Jeevan Pramaan Certificate?

Jeevan Pramaan Certificate is the Digital Life Certificate of The Government of India's Pension Scheme in Aadhaar enabled biometric authentication based digital service. It is a facility for pensioners to get their Digital Life Certificate instead of submitting a physical Life Certificate to the Pension Disbursing Agency.

Jeevan Pramaan is the Digital Life Certificate for Pensioners issued under the Aadhaar-enabled biometric authentication system by the Government of India. This certificate serves as proof of existence for pensioners and facilitates the seamless disbursement of their pension. It eliminates the need for pensioners to physically visit the pension disbursing authority.

How does Jeevan Pramaan Work?

Jeevan Pramaan Certificate being a digital service utilizes the Aadhaar platform for pensioners to be physically present at a certification authority or disbursement agency. It uses the Aadhaar platform to flag off the process of generating the Digital Life Certificate for Pensioners by facilitating biometric authentication. The pensioner can either provide his/her fingerprint or iris and avail an authentication process, and can also locate a nearby centre through the website.

Once the authentication is completed, pension disbursing agencies can directly access a pensioner's Life Certificate at the Life Certificate Repository, where it was securely stored after the completion of the process. Individuals will only have to mention their Jeevan Pramaan ID. This simplifies the process for pensioners and eliminates the need for physical submission of life certificates.

Jeevan Pramaan Life Certificate leverages the Life Certificate Repository. Both pensioners and disbursement agencies can access the digital life certificate easily at any point of time from anywhere. Jeevan Pramaan centres can be found at various locations, including government offices, banks, and post offices. Additionally, you can use the 'centre locator' on the Jeevan Pramaan website to locate a centre that is convenient for you.

Registration Process for Jeevan Pramaan

Pensioners are to follow these steps to register for the digital certificate -

  • Click on 'New Registration' on Jeevan Pramaan app.
  • Provide the requisite details pertaining to their Aadhaar card, pension order, and bank.
  • Send a Send OTP (One Time Password) to generate OTP on their Aadhaar card.
  • After generating OTP, individuals are to authenticate details via biometric verification using an Aadhaar card.

On submission, the details offered would be verified by UIDAI and a Jeevan Pramaan ID would be generated for pensioners. Registration is complete once the Jeevan Pramaan ID is generated.

How to Enrol for Jeevan Pramaan Certificate?

Pensioners can enrol for Jeevan Pramaan Certificate online by accessing either a mobile application or a PC application.

As an alternative option, individuals may visit a Jeevan Pramaan centre that is near them and get themselves registered on the spot. Several banks, Government Offices and CSCs serve as Jeevan Pramaan centres. Individuals are required to furnish essential details such as their Aadhaar card, bank details, pension order number, and registered mobile number.

Jeevan Pramaan Online Apply

  1. To generate a Jeevan Pramaan Certificate, you must first download the government Jeevan Pramaan app on your smartphone or PC.
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number, name, bank account number, pension payment order (PPO), and other personal information. There is an option that prompts the app to send an OTP.
  3. Use the OTP received to have the OTP sent to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP, which should be confirmed using Aadhaar.
  4. Once the validation is complete, click the 'Submit' button to generate a Jeevan Pramaan ID.

Who Can Benefit From It?

Individuals who avail pension from the state government, central government or other government-regulated organization would be benefited from this digitised facility. The certificate is recognised under the IT Act and eliminates the need for physical submission of life certificates.

Documents Required for the Jeevan Pramaan Patra

A pensioner would require several key documents in order to generate an online Digital life certificate or Jeevan Pramaan patra.

  • An Aadhaar card.
  • A registered and operational mobile phone number.

To generate Jeevan Pramaan Patra online, you must first register with the government Jeevan Pramaan portal. You will also need your bank account details, pension payment order, and the name of your pension disbursing agency.

Eligibility for Jeevan Pramaan

Anyone who fulfills the following conditions are eligible to obtain Jeevan Pramaan Patra -

  • Applicants should be pensioners.
  • Applicants should be a retired employee of the Central or State Government.
  • Applicants must possess a valid Aadhaar card.
  • Applicants' Aadhaar number must be registered with their respective pension disbursement agency.

Benefits of Jeevan Pramaan Certificate

The benefits of Jeevan Pramaan certificate are as follows -

  • Jeevan Pramaan eases the process of submitting Digital Life Certificate for Pensioners.
  • The feature of Aadhaar Biometric Authentication System eliminates the scope of fraudulent pertaining to pension.
  • The SMS notification keeps a Jeevan Pramaan pensioner updated about certificate generation.
  • The entire authentication process and accessing Jeevan Pramaan online is easy and feasible.
  • The ease of accessing life certificates online further accelerates the task of processing pension and directly extends timely pay-outs to Jeevan Pramaan pensioners.
