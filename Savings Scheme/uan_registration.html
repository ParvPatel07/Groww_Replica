UAN Registration

UAN or Universal Account Number is a 12-digit unique number allotted to every employee contributing to an EPF (Employees' Provident Fund) account. It acts as an umbrella for multiple Member IDs allotted to an individual by different employers. The UAN helps to centralize employee data and makes it easy to transfer/withdraw funds or track your EPF balance whenever you change jobs. This guide will cover the steps for obtaining, generating, and activating your UAN, as well as how to use it for various services.

How To Find Out Your UAN?

To understand how to find out a UAN number, there are two main ways, i.e., through employer or through the UAN portal.

Through Employer

Usually, employees are provided a Universal Account Number by their employer under the EPFO. Some employers also mention the UAN on the salary slip.

Via UAN Portal using PF Number or Member ID

If you do not have your Universal Account Number from your employer, it can also be obtained through the UAN portal as follows:

  1. Go to the UAN portal: https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/
  2. Click on 'Know your UAN' option.
  3. Enter your details such as registered mobile, Member ID, PAN, or Aadhaar number.
  4. Submit the details and an OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP and your UAN will be displayed.

How To Activate UAN Online?

To access UAN-integrated services, you must have your UAN and PAN or Aadhaar with you. The online UAN Activation process is as follows:

  1. Visit the UAN Member E-Sewa portal: https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/
  2. Click on 'Activate UAN' and enter your UAN, Aadhaar, and other details. Click 'Get Authorization PIN'.
  3. Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number and set your password.
  4. Login to the portal using your UAN and password. Under the 'Our Services' section, select 'For Employees'.
  5. Select 'Member UAN/Online services' under the services section. You will now be able to access the UAN dashboard and all online services.

How To Download Your UAN Card?

  1. Visit the EPFO member portal: https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/
  2. Login with your UAN and password.
  3. Go to the 'View' section and click on 'UAN Card'.
  4. Click on 'Download UAN Card' and save the digital card for your records.

How Can You Reset Your UAN Password for Login

  1. Go to https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/
  2. Click on 'Forgot Password' option.
  3. Enter your UAN and Captcha code.
  4. Provide your mobile number linked with the UAN.
  5. Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number.
  6. Click on the 'Submit' option.
  7. Reset your password as per your choice.

Documents Required for UAN Activation

The following documents have to be provided in order to ensure UAN Activation:

  • Aadhaar card
  • PAN card
  • Bank account details and IFSC
  • Any other proof (if asked by the employer), if required
