UAN Member Portal

The UAN Member E-Sewa portal provides a one-stop resource for accessing EPF accounts and using services online. It allows employees to check their EPF balance, update KYC, transfer or withdraw funds, download UAN card, and much more, all from a single platform without visiting the EPFO office.

What is UAN Member Portal?

Only with UAN Member E-Sewa portal, you get a wide variety of information and navigation for your EPF balance and account. You can check your passbook, download your UAN card, update KYC, transfer or withdraw funds, and track your claim status. The portal is available 24x7 and is accessible from anywhere, anytime, without a visit to the EPFO office.

This portal enables each user to manage all their 22-digit linked UANs and existing EPF/EPFO organization details in a hassle-free manner. It also allows for online KYC updates, claim status tracking, and more. To access the portal, you need to activate your UAN and complete the registration process.

Let's walk through the steps to EPFO UAN Member registration process for the EPF e-sewa portal.

UAN Member E-Sewa Portal Registration Process

  1. Visit the official UAN Member E-Sewa portal: https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/
  2. Click on the 'Activate UAN' option on the registration/login page.
  3. Enter your details: UAN, Member ID, Aadhaar, PAN, Name, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, Email, Captcha, and Authorisation PIN.
  4. After entering the details, click 'Get Authorization PIN'.
  5. Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number linked to EPFO.
  6. After verification, set your login password and activate the UAN.
  7. Now, you can log in to your registered member interface (EPFO) and access all services.
  • Note: If you forget your password, you can reset it using the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. You will need your UAN and registered mobile number to reset your password.

How to Check Your UAN Status?

  1. Go to the UAN Member portal and click on 'Know your UAN status'.
  2. Enter your details: Member ID, PAN, Aadhaar, Name, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, Captcha.
  3. Click 'Get Authorization PIN' and enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
  4. After verification, your UAN status will be displayed on the screen and sent to your registered mobile number.
  • Tip: If you forget your UAN, you can retrieve it using your Member ID, PAN, or Aadhaar number on the portal.

UAN Member E-Sewa Services

  • Profile: This section lets you view and update your complete EPF profile. It includes your contact information, KYC status, UAN, Member ID, Name, Date of Birth, and other details. Some of the fields are editable, while others are for reference only.
  • Service History: After the organization you have worked with an organization ends, you get a service history/transfer section. Here, you can check your previous PF accounts, transfer status, and other details pertaining to Employee Pension Schemes.
  • UAN Card: Here, you can view and download your UAN Card. This is helpful for EPF-related services to be carried out offline.
  • EPF Passbook: This is the passbook of your EPF. Using it, you can track the amount of the share and employer share throughout the year. The portal also describes the transaction schedule in detail.
  • Manage: Basic details. This section deals with changing your basic details, updating service information (like education, etc.).
  • KYC: Add New KYC. Current details can be updated in this section. These details include PAN, Bank Details, Passport Number, Driving License, etc.
  • Update: You can update your contact information along with KYC details and the same should be verified by the employer. KYC details will get changed automatically.

Offline Services

  • Claim (Form 31, 19, 10C): This section deals with the withdrawal of EPF. The EPFO (Employees' Provident Fund Organisation) allows you to claim your funds online as well as download the PF withdrawal form.
  • Transfer: This section allows you to transfer your PF from a previous employer to a new employer. You can transfer the funds for the purpose of claiming benefits and services through Composite Claim Form. Your Aadhaar must be linked to use UAN services.
  • Track Claim Status: You can track the status of your EPF claim, from submission to approval, using the UAN portal. This section primarily deals with what is under process.

UAN Customer Care

For any issues or queries related to your UAN Member Portal, you can contact UAN Customer Care through the following options:

  • Email: employeefeedback@epfindia.gov.in
  • Helpdesk: https://epfigms.gov.in/
  • EPFO Toll-Free: 1800 118 005
  • For more help, visit the official website: www.epfindia.gov.in
  • Note: The helpdesk is available 24x7, and you can also submit a grievance or check claim status online.
