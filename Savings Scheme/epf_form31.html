EPF Form 31 - Partial Withdrawal

EPF Form 31 is utilized to file a claim for partial withdrawal of funds from EPF or Employee’s Provident Fund. EPF is a mandatory retirement savings scheme for salaried employees in India. This form allows members to withdraw a portion of their accumulated savings to meet significant financial needs under specific circumstances.

In this particular of provident fund, employees are required to contribute a portion (12%) of their basic pay (3/5ths) each month towards their EPF account, and the employer also contributes a matching 12%. These contributions accumulate over time with interest.

The accumulated amount in the EPF account can be partially withdrawn using Form 31 during the employment period, to cover various expenses that might arise, without having to wait until retirement or job change.

When can EPF Funds be Withdrawn?

Individuals can make use of EPF withdrawal using form 31 to withdraw their EPF funds partially or in full under certain conditions. Here are some situations when partial withdrawal is allowed:

Situations Withdrawal Limit Service Eligibility Other Conditions
1. Education Individuals can withdraw contributions to EPF. 7 years Individuals can withdraw for the education of their children. The amount is limited to the expenses incurred for education or the expenses required for the education of their children’s siblings.
2. Marriage Contribution to EPF. Amount is limited to 50%. 7 years Funds can be withdrawn for the marriage of self, son/daughter, or sibling, son or daughter.
3. Land purchase or construction of a new house For land, up to 24 times of wages. For house, up to 36 times of wages. Limited to construction expenses and deemed withdrawals. 5 years Land or house must be jointly owned with spouse or family members. Funds can be withdrawn to purchase land or property, or for house construction.
4. Illness Individuals can withdraw up to 6 months of basic pay and dearness allowance. Any time Funds can be used for medical expenses for self or family members.
5. House renovation Amount withdrawn can be up to 12 times of individual's monthly wages. 5 years The house must be owned by the individual or jointly owned with spouse. Funds can be withdrawn for renovation or repair of the house.
6. Some bodily impairment Individuals can choose to withdraw up to 90% of employer's contribution to EPF. 1 year If property purchased should be registered under individual’s name, they should furnish the required documents. In case of transfer, they should furnish the required documents. In case of death, legal heirs of deceased individual can withdraw from EPF account.
7. Before retirement As much as up to 90% of the total EPF corpus. After 54 years or within one year of retirement. Limited to those who intend to retire within one year.

Individuals can apply for premature withdrawal from their EPF account, under the circumstances mentioned above, both online and offline.

How to Download EPF Form 31

Form 31 can be found on the EPF website. The process is as follows:

  1. Visit the official EPF website and go to the ‘Online Services’ menu, and then click ‘Claim’.
  2. Go to your online account, the member must first navigate to the Online Services menu and then click ‘Claim’.
  3. Go to the next page and log in with your UAN and password.
  4. Fill out the withdrawal form, providing all required details, such as personal and employment details, reason for withdrawal, amount required, bank account details, etc.
  5. The part of claim that the member wishes to apply for will be displayed on the next page. In the drop down option, choose PF Advance (Form 31).
  6. The member must tick on the purpose of the advance in the drop down list. You will also see alternatives such as illness, natural disasters, power outage, non-receipt of earnings, and purchase of disability equipment, in the drop down list. Please select the relevant option based on your requirement.
  7. After completing this, the member must sign the disclaimer. When you check the box, the ‘Get Aadhaar OTP’ button will appear. When he or she clicks that, he or she will receive the OTP, which must be authenticated. The member will receive an SMS notification for the online EPF advance application by entering the OTP and clicking on “Validate OTP and Submit Claim Form”.

The Procedure of Filing for Withdrawal through EPF Form 31

To withdraw funds from their EPF, individuals can choose to follow either one of the following two procedures:

  1. They can download and fill a physical application form (Form 31).
  2. They can submit the PF advance form 31 online and make an application.

Submission of Physical Application through Form 31

For physical submission, individuals can download Form 31 from EPFO's official website and fill up the required details. Submit it to their respective jurisdictional EPFO office after getting it attested from their employer.

Following are a few requisite details required to be filled by the employee in the EPF Form 31:

  • Contact information.
  • Purpose of advance withdrawal.
  • Amount of required advance.
  • Identity details.
  • PF account number.
  • Monthly basic pay + dearness allowance.
  • Mode of remittance.
  • Bank account details, etc.

This employer, in turn, needs to fill in the following details in case of offline submission of withdrawal form:-

  • Certification.
  • Designation, date and signature.
  • Enclosures.

Further, the EPF commissioner will need to submit the following requisite details:-

  • Account number.
  • Amount to be reimbursed.
  • Mode of remittance.
  • The officer's signature.

Submission of Online Application for Withdrawal from EPF

With the newly introduced EPF Form 31 online submission process under EPFO, filing a withdrawal claim from EPF has become significantly simpler.

However, to apply for fund withdrawal via the EPF Portal, individuals must ensure that they comply with the following criteria:

  • Their UAN or Universal Account Number is active and the mobile number linked to it is functional.
  • The UAN is linked to their KYC documents, including Aadhaar, PAN card and other requisite details.

After meeting the conditions above, individuals need to follow the steps below to submit their EPF Form 31 online:-

  1. Log in to the UAN portal through the official government website.
  2. Check if the required KYC details are updated and verified.
  3. Once the KYC details are verified, visit the “Online Service” tab and choose claim Form 31.
  4. Next, Fill in details like KYC, service details, etc. and complete the verification of the “Claim” process.
  5. Accept the certificate of undertaking to proceed.
  6. Process for online claim and select the PF withdrawal option.
  7. Select the “PF Advance form” and provide details like the purpose of withdrawal, the amount required and your current address.
  8. Next, you have to apply by clicking on the “Submit” button.

After applying, the claim request may have to wait for the employer to approve the request and draw subsequently. Once the fund is credited to their bank account, they can inquire about the status of their claim. Individuals can refer to the EPF Form 31 claim status from the official EPFO website.
