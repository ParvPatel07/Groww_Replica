EPF Forms and Their Purposes

The Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) requires members to submit various forms for different purposes related to their Provident Fund (PF) and Employees' Pension Scheme (EPS) accounts. Here is a table listing some of the common EPF forms and their uses:

Form Purpose
Form 2 Declaration and nomination form.
Form 5 New employees registering for EPF.
Form 10C Pension withdrawal.
Form 10D Pension withdrawal application after retirement.
Form 11 Auto transfer of EPF account.
Form 13 EPF account transfer.
Form 14 Withdrawal for contribution to LIC policy.
Form 19 EPF withdrawal.
Form 20 EPF in case of death of a member.
Form 31 Advances/Withdrawal.
Form 5IF Insurance claim after a member's demise.
