EPF Form 2

EPF Form 2 is a composite form used by members of the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) for both nomination and declaration purposes. It is mandatory for all EPF members to submit this form.

What is EPF Form 2?

EPF Form 2 is a two-part form. Part A is a declaration by the member regarding their family details. Part B is a nomination by the member for receiving the Provident Fund (PF) and Employees' Pension Scheme (EPS) benefits in case of the member's death.

Purpose of EPF Form 2

The main purposes of EPF Form 2 are:

  • To declare family details for the purpose of receiving benefits under the EPF and EPS schemes.
  • To nominate a person(s) to receive the accumulated PF balance and EPS pension in case of the member's demise.

Who Should Fill EPF Form 2?

Every member of the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) scheme is required to fill and submit EPF Form 2. This includes both new and existing members. It is advisable to fill this form at the time of joining the employment.

Details to be Filled in EPF Form 2

EPF Form 2 requires the following details to be filled in by the applicant:

Part A (Declaration):

  1. Member's Name
  2. Father's Name or Husband's Name (for married women)
  3. Date of Birth
  4. Marital Status
  5. Details of family members (Name, Address, Relationship, Age)

Part B (Nomination):

  1. Name of the nominee(s)
  2. Address of the nominee(s)
  3. Relationship with the member
  4. Age of the nominee(s)
  5. Percentage of share to be paid to each nominee (in case of multiple nominees)
  6. Guardian's details (if the nominee is a minor)

How to Fill and Submit EPF Form 2

EPF Form 2 can be filled and submitted both online and offline.

Offline Submission:

Download EPF Form 2 from the EPFO website. Fill in all the required details accurately in both Part A and Part B. Attach the necessary documents, such as identity proof and address proof. Submit the filled form along with the documents to your employer, who will then attest and submit it to the concerned EPFO office.

Online Submission:

Log in to the EPFO member portal using your UAN and password. Navigate to the 'Manage' tab and select 'E-Nomination'. Provide your family declaration details in Part A. Then, provide the details of your nominee(s) in Part B. You can add multiple nominees and specify the percentage of share for each. Submit the form online using your Aadhaar linked mobile number for e-sign.

Documents Required for EPF Form 2

The following documents are generally required for submitting EPF Form 2:

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport)
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License, Utility Bills)

Importance of EPF Form 2

Submitting EPF Form 2 is crucial to ensure that your family members or nominees receive the EPF and EPS benefits in case of any unforeseen event. It simplifies the claim process for the beneficiaries and avoids any disputes. It is a mandatory requirement for all EPF members.
