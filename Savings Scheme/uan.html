Universal Account Number (UAN)

What is UAN Number?

UAN or Universal Account Number is a 12-digit number allotted to employees who contribute a part of their income towards the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF). This number helps in managing multiple PF accounts under one umbrella and simplifies various processes related to the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO).

Getting a UAN Number

UAN can be availed at any position in a job, through an employer, or through the UAN portal. Usually, employees are provided specific UAN numbers by their employers. Alternatively, you can get it by visiting the EPF Member Portal or by linking UAN with your Aadhaar.

Find and Activate UAN Number

If you are searching for how to find your UAN number and activate it, you can use the EPFO homepage and click on 'Know your UAN status'. You can also activate your UAN via the EPFO portal using your registered mobile number, PAN, Aadhaar, and other details.

Download UAN Card

Visit the EPFO official website and access your UAN number, password, Captcha Code displayed. Sign in and navigate to 'View' section, then click 'UAN Card' and select 'Download UAN card' from the options.

Documents Required to Open UAN

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport, etc.)
  • Address proof (Aadhaar card, passport, utility bill, etc.)
  • Bank account details
  • ESIC card (if applicable)
  • Previous employment details (if any)

Services Offered by the Portal

  • Checking EPF balance
  • Updating KYC details
  • Transferring PF accounts
  • Withdrawing PF amount
  • Downloading UAN card

EPF Management Through UAN

  • Easy management of multiple PF accounts by linking them with UAN
  • Online withdrawal, transfer, and checking of EPF balance
  • Tracking of monthly contributions and status updates

Importance of Universal Account Number

  • Helps in easy transfer and withdrawal of PF
  • Acts as a single point of reference for all PF accounts
  • Reduces dependency on employers for PF-related services

UAN Benefits to the Employee

  • Easy access to PF account and services
  • Transparency in PF transactions
  • Quick transfer and withdrawal process

How to Withdraw or Transfer with the UAN Number?

  • Log in to the EPFO portal using your UAN and password
  • Complete KYC verification
  • Submit withdrawal or transfer request online

UAN Customer Support

EPFO provides a dedicated UAN helpdesk for queries and support. Members can also track their status, raise complaints, and get assistance through the EPFO portal or helpline numbers.
